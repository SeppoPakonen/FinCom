# Document Standard SFS 2487

This guide is based on the SFS 2487 standard and defines the layout, tabs and structure of multi-page documents.

---

## 1. Page Settings and Margins

- **Top margin:** 1 cm
- **Bottom margin:** at least 1 cm (recommended 2 cm)
- **Left margin:** 2 cm
- **Right margin:** at least 0.7 cm (recommended 1 cm)
- **Standard tabs:** Tabs are placed at 23 mm intervals measured from the left edge of the form.
    - **Tab positions (cm):** C0: 0, C1: 2.3, C2: 4.6, C3: 6.9, C4: 9.2, C5: 11.5, C6: 13.8, C7: 16.1.

---

## 2. Document Parts and Placement

Row numbers according to the standard must be adjusted according to the number of rows in the header. Word starts row numbering from 1 after the header.

### Header (Example with 6-row header)
1. **Publisher/Sender:** Written in bold (except for private individuals). Position: C0.
2. **Document name:** Starting with a capital letter (e.g. *Offer*, *Order*). Position: C3.
3. **Page number:** Included only in multi-page documents in the format `PageNo (TotalPages)`, e.g. `1 (5)`. Position: C6.
4. **Document supplement:** Clarifying information, e.g. *Certificate of Employment – Temporary*. Position: C3.

### Recipient and Headings
- **Recipient:** According to the standard, begins on row 7. If the header is 6 rows, write after the header on row 1.
- **Reference:** Standard row 14. For example, *Your advertisement in HS on 1.11.2009*.
- **Main heading:** Standard row 16. Written in bold.
- **Text:** According to the standard, begins on row 20. Text is indented to tab position C2 (4.6 cm).

---

## 3. Dates and Information

- **Date:** In domestic correspondence, use the format `day.month.year` (e.g. 4.11.2009). No leading zeros are used.
- **Contact information:** Organization name, address (PO box/street address), phone, fax, email, website, Business ID and registered office are usually placed in the header or footer in columns according to the standard.

---

## 4. Closing Entries

Space is left at the end of the document for signatures and possible additional information:

1. **Closing greeting:** 2 line breaks after the text.
2. **Publisher/Sender:** 2 line breaks after the greeting, written in CAPITAL LETTERS.
3. **Signature and name clarification:** 3–5 line breaks after the sender's name. The position/title is written below the name clarification.
4. **Additional entries:** The words *Attachment*, *Distribution* and *CC* are written at least one row below the name clarification.
    - Listed attachments are written with a capital first letter.

---

## 5. Footer

- There must be at least 3 empty rows before the footer.
- Information is usually written in a smaller font than the main text.
