**1. Q: What notifications and applications does mandatory electronic filing concern?**

All notifications and applications sent to PRH starting from 1.1.2026. These include, among others:

* Establishment notifications
* Change notifications
* Cessation notifications
* Applications
* Financial statements
* Corrections and supplements to Trade Register notifications

**2. Q: I am a sole trader. Do I have to make a notification electronically starting in 2026?**

No, you don't. Sole traders (private entrepreneurs) as well as associations and foundations registered in the Trade Register may make notifications on paper forms.

**3. Q: Why can't I file via email?**

Email does not require strong electronic authentication, unlike the YTJ service and online forms.

**4. Q: What happens if I send a notification by mail in 2026?**

We will not process paper notifications and applications received starting from 1.1.2026, unless the business form is a sole trader, association, or foundation.

**5. Q: I made a paper notification and received a request to file via the YTJ service. I had already paid the processing fee and now I had to pay again via the YTJ service. Can I get the extra payment refunded?**

Yes, you can. You received a request to make the notification in the YTJ service. The request contains detailed instructions.

**6. Q: I had already paid the processing fee but made the notification via the YTJ service anyway and had to pay again. Can I get the extra payment refunded?**

Yes, you can. Request a refund by sending the payment receipt and refund details to ilmoitusmaksut(at)prh.fi

**7. Q: I need to correct a Trade Register notification. Does the correction need to be made electronically?**

Yes, it does, if you sent the notification in 2026 and you are not a sole trader. You can send the correction or supplement in the YTJ service. Follow the instructions in the correction request you receive.

**8. Q: Does the notification need to be corrected or supplemented electronically if I sent my notification on paper in November 2025?**

No, it doesn't. Follow the instructions in the correction request you receive.

**9. Q: I need to correct the financial statements electronically. What do I do?**

Report the financial statements completely again in the YTJ service. Read the instructions [on supplementing financial statements.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/annualaccounts/how_to_supplement_and_correct_directly_submitted_annual_accounts_to_the_trade_register.html)

**10. Q: None of the responsible persons have a Finnish personal identity code. What do we do?**

Citizens of EU countries can make the notification electronically with the online form "Foreign national's identification method - notification to the Trade Register". Read more [in the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/trade-register-mandatory-online-filing/special-situations.html)

**11. Q: None of the responsible persons have Finnish or other EU authentication tools and no possibility to obtain them. What do we do?**

In this situation, an authorized contact person makes the notification. The authorization is given by someone among the company's responsible persons, for example, a board member or managing director. Read more [in the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/trade-register-mandatory-online-filing/special-situations.html)

**12. Q: Our company's responsible partners are companies. What do we do?**

A person authorized to represent the company, for example, the managing director, makes the notification via the online form. Reporting for these companies in the YTJ service will become possible during 2026. Read more [in the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/trade-register-mandatory-online-filing/special-situations.html)

**13. Q: I don't have online banking credentials to make the notification electronically. What do I do?**

Authorize a contact person to make the notification on your behalf. Read more [in the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/trade-register-mandatory-online-filing/special-situations.html)

**14. Q: Can an attorney make the notification on behalf of the responsible persons?**

They can prepare the notification ready for signing in the YTJ service. After that, the responsible person signs the notification. Read more [about signing in the YTJ service on the ytj.fi website. Opens in a new tab](https://www.ytj.fi/en/notification/how-to-make-electronic-notification/what-you-need-for-electronic-notification/signing-a-notification.html)

**15. Q: I don't speak Finnish or Swedish, so I can't use the YTJ service or online forms. What do I do?**

The official languages of the Trade Register are Finnish and Swedish. You can ask for help from [Business Finland advisory service Opens in a new tab](https://www.suomi.fi/instructions-and-support/support-and-advice/business-finland-advisory-service) or [regional new enterprise center. Opens in a new tab](https://uusyrityskeskus.fi/)

You can also authorize a contact person to make the notification on your behalf. Read more [in the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/trade-register-mandatory-online-filing/special-situations.html)
