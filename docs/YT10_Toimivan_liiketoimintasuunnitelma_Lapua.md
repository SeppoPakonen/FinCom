# Template: Business Plan (Established Company)

This template is intended for developing an already operating company and supporting financing applications.

## 1. Basic Information
* **Company name:** ____________________________________
* **Author and date:** ____________________________________

## 2. Operating Environment and Competitor Analysis
* **Industry:** [Description of main industry and market area]
* **Competitors:** List main competitors (name, location, strengths).
* **Our competitive advantages:** Why would a customer choose us over our competitors?

## 3. Vision and Strategy
* **Vision (10 years):** Where is the company in the long term?
* **Goals (5 years):** Revenue, profitability, number of employees.
* **Strategy:** Concrete methods for achieving the goals.

## 4. Marketing and Sales
* **Customer segments:** Who do we sell to?
* **Channels:** How do we reach new and existing customers?
* **Budget:** How much will we spend on marketing in the upcoming financial year?

## 5. Production and Responsibility
* **Investments:** Required machines, equipment and software.
* **Environmental impact:** Energy consumption, waste and recyclability of products.
* **Supply chain:** How do we select suppliers (locality, ethics)?

## 6. Personnel and Product Development
* **Team:** Number, expertise and training needs.
* **R&D:** Plans for new products and services.

## 7. Finances and Risks
* **Profitability:** Based on YT22 calculations (attachment).
* **Risk management:** Identified threats and methods for minimizing them.
