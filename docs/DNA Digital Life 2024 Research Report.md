# DNA Digital Life 2024 - Research Report

This document is a condensed summary of a study conducted by DNA in 2024, which describes Finnish people's digital lifestyle, device usage, and attitudes.

---

## 1. Background and Method of the Study

- **Objective:** To understand and describe Finnish people's digital lifestyle (study series started in 2017).
- **Target group:** All Finnish people over 15 years old (upper age limit removed in 2024).
- **Data collection:** March 18–27, 2024 from Nepa's online panels.
- **Number of respondents:** 1018 people (weighted to represent the population by age and gender).

---

## 2. Smart Device Usage and Prevalence

### Phones
- **Smartphone ownership:** 98% of Finns own a smartphone.
- **5G technology:** 56% have a 5G phone in use (significant growth from 2021, when the share was 17%).
- **Age differences:** 5G usage is prominent among those under 55, but smartphones are common in all age groups (min. 95%).

### Other devices in households (Top 5)
1. Laptop computer: 82%
2. Smart TV: 61%
3. Tablet computer: 58%
4. Desktop computer: 46%
5. Gaming console: 42%

**Trends:** Smart TV and smartwatch usage is increasing, while regular TV and recording digital boxes are decreasing.

---

## 3. Used Applications and Services

Application usage has increased in almost all areas.

### Most Popular Application Types (2024):
- **Banking services:** 83%
- **Communication (e.g., WhatsApp):** 77%
- **Maps and routes:** 77%
- **Social media:** 75%
- **Mobile payment (e.g., MobilePay):** 65% (+7 percentage points growth from the previous year)
- **Shopping cart applications:** 53% (+17 percentage points significant growth)

### Age Group Differences in Applications:
- **Under 25 years:** Entertainment is emphasized (music, streaming, Snapchat).
- **Older age groups:** Utility applications are emphasized (banks, maps, security).
- **25–34 years:** Use all application types evenly, especially mobile payment.

---

## 4. Social Media Usage

### Daily Usage (Top 5):
1. **WhatsApp:** 70% (most active in all age groups)
2. **Facebook:** 61%
3. **Instagram:** 40%
4. **YouTube (free version):** 38%
5. **Snapchat:** 25%

**Notes:** Instagram's and especially TikTok's popularity is rising. Half of Finns use WhatsApp numerous times daily.

---

## 5. Future Outlook and Artificial Intelligence

- **Artificial Intelligence:** 49% believe they will use AI (e.g., ChatGPT) regularly or occasionally within the next two years. Among under 25-year-olds, the share is 90%.
- **Mobile Payment:** Usage is expected to continue growing (78% believe they will use it in the future).

---

## 6. Attitudes Toward Digitalization

- **Benefits:** Digital services are perceived as meaningful, and people want to handle matters online.
- **Challenges:** 
    - 39% feel digital services hinder concentration.
    - 38% find it difficult to disconnect from the internet.
    - 31% are interested in buying a used smartphone.
- **Change:** Attitudes toward rapidly adopting new services have slightly cooled compared to the previous year.
