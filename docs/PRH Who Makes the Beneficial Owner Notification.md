# PRH: Who makes the beneficial owner notification?

Making a beneficial owner notification is a statutory obligation for most business forms. The notification must be made even if there are no beneficial owners or if they are unknown.

## 1. Business forms that always make the notification
* Limited liability company and public limited liability company.
* Cooperative.
* Savings bank and cooperative bank.
* European company.
* Insurance association and insurance company.

## 2. Business forms that notify only when necessary
* **Open company and limited partnership:** Notification is usually made only if the beneficial owner is someone other than a partner (e.g., a company is the general partner).

## 3. Business forms that do NOT make the notification
* Stock exchange companies.
* **Sole traders** (Private entrepreneurs).
* Housing companies and mutual real estate companies.
* Associations and foundations.
* Religious communities.

---
**Note:** For branch offices, the foreign parent company is responsible for notification according to its home country's legislation.
