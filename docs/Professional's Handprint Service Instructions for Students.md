# Professional's Handprint Service: Instructions for Students

Welcome to study responsible and sustainable practices in your field. This guide assists you in using the service.

---

## 1. Login Credentials and Sign-in

Your teacher has two options to add you to the course:

### 1.1. Registering to a group via link
Your teacher shares a link (e.g., via email or Moodle).
1. Click the link.
2. Enter the required information.
3. Click **Create credentials and join the group**.
4. You are directed to the *My Courses* page and can start studying.

### 1.2. Logging in with teacher-created credentials
If the teacher has created credentials for you, you have received an email containing login information.
1. Log in at: [https://kadenjalki.fi/kirjaudu/](https://kadenjalki.fi/kirjaudu/).
2. Enter your email address and the password you received in the email.
3. **Note!** Be careful not to copy extra spaces into the password.
4. If you forget the password, use the **Forgot password?** function.
5. Change the password on the first login on the *Profile* page.

---

## 2. Profile Information and Management

You can find your information in the upper right corner under **Profile**.

### 2.1. Editing User Information
In the profile, you can edit:
- First name, last name, and username (note: the username cannot be changed).
- Email address (should be changed after graduation to continue downloading certificates later).
- Password.
- Profile picture.

### 2.2. Achievement Certificates
You can find all your completed certificates in your profile. You can download them as PDF files to your computer.

### 2.3. Personal Data Management (GDPR)
You have the right to manage your data:
- You can request a summary of collected data.
- You can request deletion of your data if the educational institution or employer no longer requires retention of educational information.

---

## 3. Completing the Course

You can find your courses under **My Courses**.
- **Green mark:** Course is completed.
- **Blue mark:** Course is ongoing.

### 3.1. Course Structure
Courses are divided into **modules**, which consist of **lessons**.
- Studying one lesson takes approximately 5–15 minutes.
- After going through the content, click **Mark as complete**.
- If sustainability is new to you, start with the *Introduction to Sustainable Development* course.

### 3.2. Tasks and Tests
1. **Reflection tasks:** Encourage thinking about work sustainability. Do not affect passing, but the teacher may require completing them.
2. **Competency tests:** Each module ends with a test (6–12 questions).
    - Passing requirement: **80% of points**.
    - If you don't pass, you can retry immediately or return to review the material.

### 3.3. Progress Tracking
The progress bar at the top of the page shows your status. The *breadcrumb* indicates where in the course you are.

### 3.4. Receiving a Certificate
The course is completed when all competency tests are passed.
1. Complete the final test.
2. Answer the feedback survey.
3. Download the competency certificate or check your email.

---

## 4. Giving Feedback

You can provide feedback via the **Give Feedback** button on each page. Feedback is especially valuable if:
- The content was interesting or unnecessary.
- The text contains difficult words or is too challenging.
- You notice technical defects.
