# PRH: Branch Office Financial Statements

A foreign company's branch office located in Finland must submit its financial statements to the Trade Register annually.

## 1. Schedule
* **Deadline:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.
* **EEA exception:** If the parent company's home country (EEA country) law allows a longer period, that is followed.

## 2. Documents to be submitted
* **According to EU regulations:** If the parent company follows EU financial statement standards, the parent company's official financial statements are submitted (translated into an EU language).
* **Others:** If the parent company does not follow EU standards, the branch office must submit its own financial statements prepared according to Finnish law.

## 3. Notification
* **Method:** Electronically in the YTJ service.
* **Signatory:** The branch office's representative or business name signer.
* **Fee:** Free of charge.

---
**Note:** All attachments are public. Do not include personal identity codes or sensitive information in them.
