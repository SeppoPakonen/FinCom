# Guide: Demonstration Materials for Business Management Professional Qualification

This document compiles information about the materials required for the demonstration and assessment criteria.

## 1. Mandatory Parts of the Qualification
* **Working in Business Operations (30 cr):** Customer service and working in the company's daily operations.
* **Planning and Starting Business Operations (60 cr):** Business plan, financial calculations and establishment procedures.

## 2. Optional Parts (One is selected)
* **Sales and Marketing (60 cr):** Strategy, annual calendar and budget.
* **Developing Products and Services (60 cr):** Productization and piloting.

## 3. Materials Required for the Demonstration
Ensure that the following documents are uploaded to the Moodle platform before the assessment:
* **Self-assessment forms** for each part of the qualification.
* **Business plan (BP)** and **Financial calculations (YT4/YT5)**.
* **CV** and previous work certificates.
* **Responsibility plan** (sustainable development).
* **Marketing materials** (e.g. logo, brochures, social media plan).

## 4. Assessment Discussion
* **Duration:** Approximately 1 hour.
* **Participants:** Student, responsible instructor and working life representative.
* **Decision:** Pass / Fail. The assessment is based on the qualification criteria and the presented materials.
