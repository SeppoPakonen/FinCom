# PRH: Establishing a Cooperative

A cooperative is formed when the establishment notification is registered in the Trade Register.

## 1. Schedule and Responsibility
* **Deadline:** The notification must be made within **3 months** of signing the articles of incorporation.
* **Signatory:** One of the regular board members or an authorized agent.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 400 €.
* **Additional business name:** 75 €.
* Payment must be made before submitting the notification.

## 3. Required Documents
* **Forms:** Y1, attachment 2 and personal information form.
* **Attachments:** Original articles of incorporation, cooperative's rules, receipt of payment and board's declaration of compliance with the law.

## 4. Contents of Articles of Incorporation
* Date and founding members.
* Number of shares and subscription price.
* Payment deadline for shares.
* Board members (and chairperson).
* Definition of financial year.

## 5. Notification
* Made electronically using the online form at [turvaviesti.prh.fi](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin).
* Paper forms will be discontinued as of 1.1.2026.

---
**Remember:** After registration, a separate, free **beneficial owner notification** must be made in the YTJ service.
