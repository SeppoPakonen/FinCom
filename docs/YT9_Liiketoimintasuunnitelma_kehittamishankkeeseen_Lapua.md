# Template: Business Plan (Development Project)

This template is intended for companies applying for funding for a separate development project.

## 1. Current State of the Company
* Brief description: Market position, profitability, production and premises.

## 2. Changes in the Operating Environment
* How has the industry changed? (Competition, quality requirements, customer demand).

## 3. Development Actions
* **Need:** Why is the project implemented?
* **Description:** What is done in the project?
* **Impact:** How does the project improve competitiveness, employment or profitability?

## 4. Marketing and Sales Strategy (Regarding the project)
* How are new customers reached through the project?
* Key target groups.

## 5. Risk Assessment
* What risks are specifically involved in this development project?

## 6. Responsibility Statement (ESG)
* **Social:** Impact on employee well-being and the local community.
* **Economic:** Impact on regional vitality and employment.
* **Environmental:** Energy efficiency, raw material savings and product lifecycle.

## 7. Calculations
* Project financial plan and profit forecast attached.
