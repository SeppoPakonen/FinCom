# PRH: Establishing a Limited Liability Company

A limited liability company (Ltd) is formed when it is registered in the Trade Register. The establishment notification must be made electronically in the YTJ service.

## 1. Formation Methods
* **Option A (Guided package):** The service creates the documents. Suitable when share capital is 0 €, founders are adults and a standard articles of association is sufficient. Fee: **300 €**.
* **Option B (Establishment notification):** Add your own documents. Used when there is share capital, companies are shareholders or a customized articles of association is needed. Fee: **400 €**.

## 2. Required Information
* Business name (suggest 3 options), business sector and registered office.
* Board members and possible managing director.
* Ultimate beneficial owners (over 25 % ownership or voting rights).

## 3. Important Notes
* **Electronic service:** Mandatory starting from 1.1.2026.
* **Business name check:** Use the [Company Name Service](https://nimipalvelu.prh.fi) to verify name availability.
* **Minor:** Can be a shareholder under certain conditions, but not a board member.
