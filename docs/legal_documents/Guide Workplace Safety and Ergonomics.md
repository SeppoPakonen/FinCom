# Guide: Workplace Safety and Ergonomics

A good work environment is safe, healthy, and productive. Improving workplace safety is based on risk assessment and prevention.

## 1. Key Concepts
* **Occupational Health Protection:** Improving the work environment and conditions to safeguard work capacity.
* **Workplace Well-being:** Safe and productive work in a professional work community. Work is experienced as meaningful.
* **Workplace Safety:** The condition of the work environment from the perspective of safety and health.

## 2. Risk Management (e.g., retail industry)
* **Physical risks:** Demanding work positions, heavy lifting, repetitive work (e.g., cash register).
* **Environmental risks:** Electrical and fire safety, slippery surfaces, noise, and lighting.
* **Psychological risks:** Threat and violence situations, working alone, mental stress.

## 3. Ergonomics at the Workstation
* **Adjustability:** Work tools and furniture must suit the worker's height and characteristics.
* **Space:** Adequate space for work (min. 10–12 m²/person) and无障碍 pathways.
* **Display work:** Opportunity to change positions (standing desk) and appropriate lighting. The employer must provide display work glasses if regular glasses are not suitable for the work.

## 4. Lifting Technique Tips
* **Use your legs:** Bend knees and keep your back straight.
* **Close proximity:** Keep the load close to your body.
* **Activate your abdominal muscles:** They support your back during lifting.
* **Ask for help:** Don't lift heavy loads alone.

## 5. Dealing with Threatening Situations
1. **Stay calm:** Speak clearly and briefly.
2. **Give space:** Don't violate the other person's territory or self-esteem.
3. **Keep hands visible:** Don't make sudden movements.
4. **Avoid provocation:** Don't laugh or use humor.

## 6. Employer Obligations
* Arrange statutory **occupational health care**.
* Orient employees on workplace safety and machine use.
* Conduct regular risk assessments.
* Appoint an occupational safety manager (and delegate if > 10 employees).

---
**Remember:** Workplace safety is everyone's concern. Report defects and deficiencies immediately to your supervisor.
