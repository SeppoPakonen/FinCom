## Terms of Agreement

1. **Recording in balance sheet:** The invested capital is recorded in full in the company's balance sheet as an unrestricted equity investment in the company's unrestricted equity fund (other equity investment, Limited Liability Companies Act 624/2006, Section 8:2).
2. **Board decision:** The company's board decides at its meeting to accept the SVOP investment by   .  .202   and delivers to the Investor a certified copy of the meeting minutes.
3. **Accounting:** The company is obligated to maintain separate accounting records for SVOP investments and capital returns to reliably determine the tax implications of possible capital returns.
