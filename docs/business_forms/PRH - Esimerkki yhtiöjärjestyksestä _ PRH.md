# Template: Articles of Association for an Apartment Company

The articles of association of an apartment company is a public document that defines the management of the company and apartments.

## Key Sections of the Articles of Association

### Section 1 Trade Name
The company's name is: [Enter the name given in the YTJ service here].

### Section 2 Registered Office
The company's registered office is: [Municipality].

### Section 3 Business Activity (Real Estate)
Information on the real estate managed by the company (property ID, name and basis of management).

### Section 4 Unit Description
Defines which shares entitle the holder to occupy certain apartments or parking spaces.
* Example: *Building A, Apartment A1, 37 m², shares 1–10.*

### Section 5 Signing the Company Name
How the company name is signed (e.g. board members together or chairman alone).

### Section 6 Charges
Shareholders must pay maintenance and capital charges to cover the company's expenses.
* **Basis:** [e.g. square meters or number of shares].
* **Water Charge:** Often charged according to consumption.

### Section 7 Board of Directors
The company's affairs are managed by the board (1–5 regular members). Term of office ends at the next annual general meeting.

### Section 8 General Meeting
Must be held within 6 months of the end of the financial year. The meeting handles:
* Confirmation of the financial statements.
* Granting of discharge from liability.
* Board's report on future maintenance needs (five-year plan).

**Note:** Companies established before 2019 may change their articles of association to a digital structured format.
