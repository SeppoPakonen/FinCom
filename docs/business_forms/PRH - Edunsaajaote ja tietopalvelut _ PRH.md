# PRH: Beneficial Ownership Extract and Information Services

PRH provides information on beneficial owners registered in the Trade Register through beneficial ownership extracts.

## 1. What is a beneficial ownership extract?
* Contains the beneficial ownership information reported by a single company.
* Available in Finnish, Swedish and English.
* **Cost:** The extract is a paid document.

## 2. Publicity of Information and Restrictions
* Beneficial ownership information is **not public** in the same way as other Trade Register information.
* Information is disclosed only to parties that have a legitimate purpose according to the Money Laundering Act (e.g., banks, lawyers, authorities).
* **Public information:** Information on *whether* a company has filed a beneficial ownership notification is public and visible in the Virre information service.

## 3. Obtaining a Requested Extract
A beneficial ownership extract can be ordered:
1. As a single document order from PRH's online service.
2. As a contractual customer of the Virre information service.

---
**Search:** You can check the status of the notification using the Business ID in the [Virre information service](https://virre.prh.fi).
