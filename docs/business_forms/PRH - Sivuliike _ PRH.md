# PRH: Financial Statements of a Branch Office

A foreign company's branch office located in Finland must file its financial statements in the Trade Register annually.

## 1. Timeline
* **Deadline:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.
* **EEA Exception:** If the parent company's home country (EEA country) law allows more time, that time frame is followed.

## 2. Documents to be Filed
* **EU Regulation Compliant:** If the parent company follows EU financial statement standards, the parent company's official financial statements must be filed (translated into an EU language).
* **Others:** If the parent company does not follow EU standards, the branch office must file its own financial statements prepared according to Finnish law.

## 3. Filing
* **Method:** Electronically in the YTJ service.
* **Signatory:** The branch office's representative or person authorized to sign the business name.
* **Cost:** Free of charge.

---
**Note:** All attachments are public. Do not include personal identity codes or sensitive information in them.
