# Comparison: Business Forms and Taxation 2025

The choice of company form affects your liabilities, decision-making, profit distribution, and taxation.

## 1. Private entrepreneur (Trade Name)
* **Suitable for:** Single entrepreneurs, small-scale operations.
* **Liability:** Full personal liability with all property.
* **Taxation:** Profits are taxed as the entrepreneur's personal income and capital income.
* **Establishment:** Easy and affordable (about 70 €).

## 2. Personal companies (Ay and Ky)
* **General Partnership (Ay):** At least two partners. Everyone has unlimited and joint liability. Requires strong trust.
* **Limited Partnership (Ky):** At least one general partner (liable with all property) and one silent partner (investor, liable only with the invested amount).
* **Taxation:** Profits are distributed to partners for taxation according to the partnership agreement.

## 3. Limited Liability Company (Oy)
* **Suitable for:** Companies of all sizes, professional growth.
* **Liability:** The company itself is responsible for its debts. Shareholder risk is limited to the invested capital.
* **Decision-making:** General meeting and board of directors. Share capital is not mandatory (0 €).
* **Taxation:** Independent taxpayer (corporate tax 20%). Owners receive funds as salary or dividends.

## 4. Light Entrepreneurship
* Not an actual business form, but a way to invoice for work without having your own Business ID (through a service provider). Good way to test a business idea.

---
**Tip:** Choose a form that best protects you from operational risks. Limited liability company is often the safest choice for growing business operations.
