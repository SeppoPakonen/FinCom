# Tool: New Entrepreneur's Checklist

This checklist helps you ensure that all important matters are handled in the early stages of your company.

## 1. Planning and Preparation
* [ ] **Clarify your business idea:** What do you sell, to whom and how?
* [ ] **Business plan and calculations:** Create a business plan and financial calculations (YT4/YT5).
* [ ] **Expert help:** Book time for business consulting (e.g., New Enterprise Center).
* [ ] **Startup grant:** Apply for a decision BEFORE registering the company.

## 2. Official Registrations
* [ ] **Registration notification:** Submit notification at [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi) service.
* [ ] **Tax registration:** Apply for advance payment and VAT registers.
* [ ] **Trademark:** Protect the company name or logo if needed at PRH.

## 3. Bank and Insurance
* [ ] **Business bank account:** Open a bank account and get payment terminal or online payment processing.
* [ ] **YEL insurance:** Take the mandatory pension insurance from a pension company.
* [ ] **Other insurances:** Liability insurance, accident insurance and legal protection insurance.

## 4. Everyday Tools
* [ ] **Accounting:** Choose an accounting firm and agree on electronic financial management.
* [ ] **Contract templates:** Prepare templates for quotations, orders and invoices.
* [ ] **IT equipment:** Computer, phone and necessary software.

## 5. Sales and Marketing
* [ ] **Online presence:** Reserve a domain, create website and open social media channels.
* [ ] **Logo design:** Create a recognizable brand for your company.
* [ ] **Pricing:** Decide on prices for products and services.

---
**Remember:** Networking is important! Join a local entrepreneur organization or professional network.
