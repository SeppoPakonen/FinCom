# PRH: Establishing an Apartment Company

An apartment company (as-oy) is formed by registering it in the Trade Register. New companies automatically belong to the unit information system.

## 1. Timeline and Responsible Persons
* **Deadline:** The establishment notification must be made within **3 months** of signing the articles of incorporation. Delaying will result in the establishment lapsing.
* **Signatory:** One of the board members.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 400 €.
* **Auxiliary business name:** 75 € / name.

## 3. Required Attachments
1. Original, signed articles of incorporation.
2. Board members' and property manager's certificate of compliance with the law.
3. Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
4. Possible articles of association attachments (e.g., liability distribution table).
5. Copies of identity documents for persons without a Finnish personal identity code.

## 4. Notification (YTJ Service)
* Notification is made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* The articles of association are submitted in electronic form. Sections 1–4 are standardized and the service creates them based on the provided information.
* The list of shareholders is transmitted directly from the service to the National Land Survey of Finland.

## 5. Tracking
* Business ID is received immediately after submission.
* Processing can be tracked in the [Virre information service](https://virre.prh.fi).

---
**Note:** Do not attach documents containing sensitive information (e.g., health information) or home addresses unless specifically requested.
