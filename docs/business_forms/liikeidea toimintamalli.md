# Business Idea and Business Model: Strategic Checklist

This document helps analyze and develop a company's business idea and business model.

## 1. Cornerstones of the Business Idea
* [ ] **What?** What benefit or value do you provide to the customer? What problem is solved?
* [ ] **For whom?** Who is your ideal customer? Who belongs to the core group?
* [ ] **How?** With what resources and processes is the product/service created?

## 2. Competitive Advantage (What makes you better?)
* [ ] Strong and unique product portfolio.
* [ ] Competitive pricing (justified).
* [ ] Flexibility and ability to adapt to demand.
* [ ] Responsibility and sustainable operations (ESG).
* [ ] Excellent customer service.

## 3. Market Knowledge
* [ ] **Industry:** Is the industry growing or declining? What are the megatrends?
* [ ] **Competitors:** What are their strengths and weaknesses?
* [ ] **Availability:** What are the best sales channels?

## 4. Risk Management
* **Business risks:** Decision-making under uncertainty.
* **Hazard risks:** Personnel, property, information security.
* **Financial risks:** Solvency and cash flow.
* **Entrepreneur's endurance:** Personal performance capacity.

### Risk Management Methods:
1. **Avoid:** Don't take unnecessary risks.
2. **Reduce:** Plan and anticipate.
3. **Transfer:** Insurance and contracts.
4. **Accept:** Acknowledge and accept calculated risks.

## 5. Modeling Tools
Recommended tools for visualizing the business idea:
* **BMC** (Business Model Canvas)
* **Lean Canvas**
* **Business Plan**

---
*The business idea can and should change during operations. "It's not counted until it's in the market."*
