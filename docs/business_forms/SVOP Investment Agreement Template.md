## Terms of the Agreement

1. **Recording in the balance sheet:** The capital to be invested is recorded in its entirety in the company's balance sheet as a gratuitous investment in the invested free equity capital fund (other equity investment, Limited Liability Companies Act 624/2006, Section 8:2).
2. **Board decision:** The company's board decides at its meeting to accept the SVOP investment by   .  .202   and delivers a certified copy of the meeting minutes to the Investor.
3. **Accounting:** The company is obligated to maintain separate accounting for SVOP investments and capital returns so that the tax effects of possible capital returns can be reliably determined.
