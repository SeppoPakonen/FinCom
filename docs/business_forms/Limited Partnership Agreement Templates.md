# Limited Partnership Agreement (Templates)

This document contains templates for establishing a limited partnership (Ky). In a limited partnership, there is at least one **general partner** and one **silent partner**.

---

## Filling Instructions and Notes
- Fill in the required sections (trade name, registered office, industry, contributions).
- Remember signatures after printing.
- **Shareholder/Partnership Agreement:** Recommended if there is more than one general partner.
- **Data Protection:** Personal information (social security number, address) is reported on a separate form to the Trade Register.
- **Disclaimer:** Templates are examples; the author is not liable for their suitability in individual cases.

### 4. Chief Executive Officer and Representation
- A chief executive officer may be appointed for ongoing administration.
- The general partner represents the company.

### 5. Profit Distribution
- A predetermined annual interest rate is paid to the silent partner from profits on the capital contribution paid (payment by the end of January).
- If profits are insufficient, the shortfall is paid from the following year's profits.
- The portion exceeding the profit share is distributed to the general partner.

### 6. Death of a Partner and Transfer of Share
- The company does not dissolve upon death; heirs take the place (requires 18 years of age).
- Redemption price: Company's assets - liabilities, reduced by 30 percent.
- **Transfer Right:** The silent partner may not transfer their share without the consent of the general partner.

### 7. Dissolution
- Notice period: 6 months.
- Upon dissolution, after debts, the silent partner's contribution and interest are paid first; the remainder goes to the general partner.

## TEMPLATE 2: Two General Partners and One Silent Partner

*(Like Template 1, with the following additions)*

- **Relationship between General Partners:** Both general partners have the right to represent the company and handle its affairs unless otherwise agreed.
- **Profit Distribution:** After the silent partner's interest, the remaining profit is divided between the general partners according to their contributions or a separate agreement.
- **Dissolution:** If one general partner gives notice to terminate the agreement, the other may continue operations by redeeming the departing party's share.
