# General Instructions for Trade Register Notifiers

From the menu, you can find instructions that are common to several or all business forms.

Also familiarize yourself with the business form-specific notification instructions, for example, those concerning limited liability companies. [Familiarize yourself with the limited liability company instructions.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/establishingacompany/establishinglimitedliabilitycompany.html)

## Be careful with personal identity codes and home addresses

Don't provide personal identity codes and home addresses unless specifically requested. Don't attach documents containing personal identity codes, home addresses, or sensitive information such as health information to notifications. [Familiarize yourself with the processing of personal information at PRH.](https://www.prh.fi/en/about_prh/dataprotection.html)
