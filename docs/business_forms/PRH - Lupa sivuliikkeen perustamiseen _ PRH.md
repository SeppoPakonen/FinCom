# PRH: Permission to Establish a Branch Office

If a foreign company's registered office is outside the EEA area, it needs permission from PRH to establish a branch office in Finland.

## 1. Granting of Permission
Permission is usually granted if the parent company is properly registered in its home country.

## 2. Branch Representative
* The branch must have an **representative** who has the right to receive service of process on behalf of the company.
* If the parent company is from the EEA area, the representative's place of residence must be in the EEA area.
* If the parent company is from outside the EEA area, the representative's domicile must be **in Finland**.

## 3. Application
* **Method:** Online form at [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin).
* **Fee:** 150 € (+ 6.50 € invoice fee).
* **Attachments:**
    - Informal application.
    - Certificate of registration of the parent company.
    - Copy of articles of association.
    - Certified translations to Finnish or Swedish.

## 4. Next Steps
After receiving permission, you must make the actual **branch establishment notification** to the Trade Register and attach the permit decision.
