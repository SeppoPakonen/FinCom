# My Digital Environment

**Author:** Seppo Pakonen
**Date:** November 2, 2025

This document describes the technology, software and information gathering methods I use in my daily routine.

## 1. Daily Equipment
* **Computer:** Powerful custom workstation (equivalent in performance to a 2021 MacBook Pro).
* **Mobile device:** OnePlus 9 Pro smartphone.

## 2. Operating Systems
I use a variety of environments depending on the need:
* **Gentoo Linux (Plasma):** My primary operating system due to its speed and customizability.
* **Windows 11:** For traditional software needs.
* **macOS Sequoia (Hackintosh):** To utilize the Apple ecosystem.
* **FreeBSD (Plasma):** Unix-based alternative.

## 3. Social Media and Information Gathering
* **Used platforms:** Threads, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X (formerly Twitter), Gettr, VK.
* **Primary use:** Writing to Threads and updating music artist profiles.
* **News sources:**
    - **Technology:** Hacker News (Web & Android).
    - **General news:** Wikipedia: Current Events.
    - **Tool:** RSS feeds (using Feeder Android app).

## 4. Workflow and Philosophy
* **PC focus:** I prefer to do work on a large screen rather than on a phone.
* **Flexibility:** I use the best tool for each task, regardless of operating system.
* **Information management:** I aim for curated and efficient information gathering while avoiding unnecessary information overload.
