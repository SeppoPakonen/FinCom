# PRH: Open Company (Ay) and Limited Partnership (Ky)

A notification must always be made to the Trade Register about the establishment of an open company and limited partnership. The company is formed by registration.

## 1. Establishment Process
* **Deadline:** The notification must be submitted **3 months** after signing the partnership agreement.
* **Signing:** 
    - **Ay:** One of the partners.
    - **Ky:** One of the general partners.
    - Procuration does not authorize signing the notification.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 300 €.
* **Auxiliary trade name:** 75 € / name.
* The fee must be paid before submitting the notification.

## 3. Required Documents
* **Y2 form** and separate **personal information form** (contains SSNs and addresses).
* Original partnership agreement.
* Statement about the appointment of managing director or procura (if appointed).
* Receipt of processing fee.

## 4. Minor as a Partner
A person under 18 can be a partner if:
1. Guardians represent them.
2. Guardians have permission from the **DVV** (Digital and Population Data Services Agency) for the action.

## 5. Reporting Financial Period
The financial period must be reported to the Trade Register if the general partner is a limited liability company or another Ay/Ky, whose general partner is a limited liability company.
