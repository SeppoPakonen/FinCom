# Service: New Business Centers' Advisory Services

New Business Centers offer free, confidential, and impartial advice to all interested in entrepreneurship throughout Finland.

## 1. Personal Business Consultation
Meet an experienced business advisor who coaches you in:
* **Developing the business idea:** Is your idea sustainable and profitable?
* **Making calculations:** Sales, financial, and profitability calculations.
* **Choosing a business form:** Which one suits your situation?
* **Applying for funding:** Information about startup grants and banks.

## 2. Other Services
* **Entrepreneurship information:** A good way to start and get basic information at once.
* **Idea pump:** Test your idea online and receive a preliminary evaluation.
* **Establishment guide:** Comprehensive information package for the first steps of entrepreneurship.
* **Networks:** A business advisor can connect you to experts (lawyers, accountants, insurance professionals).

## 3. Monetary Benefits
Companies established through New Business Centers receive significant benefits from partners, for example:
* **Financial management:** Discounts on Netvisor or Holvi services.
* **Contracts:** Legal document templates and electronic signature (e.g., Docue).
* **E-commerce:** Trial periods and discounts (e.g., MyCashflow).

## 4. Why Use New Business Centers?
* **Certainty:** According to studies, 80% of companies using advisory services are still operating after 5 years (compared to only 50% of other companies).
* **Free of charge:** The service is free for entrepreneurs with support from municipalities and partner companies.
* **Quality:** The operations have an ISO 9001 quality certificate.
