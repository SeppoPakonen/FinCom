# T7: Non-Disclosure Agreement (Business Transaction)

This is a template for a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) used during the preparation phase of a business transaction to protect information being disclosed.

---

**PARTIES**
1. **Disclosing Party:** [Company name], [Business ID]
2. **Receiving Party:** [Company name / Person], [Business ID / Personal identity code]

**1. PURPOSE OF THE AGREEMENT**
The parties are negotiating a possible business transaction or business arrangement (hereinafter "the Arrangement"). The Disclosing Party will provide the Receiving Party with confidential material for evaluation of the Arrangement.

**2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION**
Confidential information includes all information related to the Disclosing Party's business operations, such as financial figures, customer lists, contracts, strategies, technical information, and even the existence of the negotiations themselves.

**3. OBLIGATION TO MAINTAIN CONFIDENCE**
The Receiving Party commits to:
* Keeping the information strictly confidential.
* Using the information solely for evaluating the Arrangement.
* Disclosing information only to its own employees or advisors who need to know it for implementing the Arrangement.
* Ensuring that the aforementioned parties also comply with this agreement.

**4. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF INFORMATION**
If the Arrangement does not materialize or if the Disclosing Party so requests, the Receiving Party must immediately return or demonstrably destroy all confidential material received, including copies.

**5. PENALTY AND DAMAGES**
If the Receiving Party breaches this agreement, it is obligated to pay the Disclosing Party a penalty of [Amount] euros. The penalty does not limit the Disclosing Party's right to claim full compensation for damages exceeding the penalty amount.

**6. VALIDITY**
This agreement enters into force upon signing and remains valid for [Number, e.g. 3] years from the disclosure of the material, regardless of whether the Arrangement is realized or not.

**7. SIGNATURES**
