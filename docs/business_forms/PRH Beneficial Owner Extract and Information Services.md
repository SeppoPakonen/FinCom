# PRH: Beneficial Owner Extract and Information Services

PRH provides information on beneficial owners registered in the Trade Register through a beneficial owner extract.

## 1. What is a beneficial owner extract?
* Contains beneficial owner information reported by a single company.
* Available in Finnish, Swedish, and English.
* **Cost:** The extract is a paid document.

## 2. Publicity of Information and Restrictions
* Beneficial owner information is **not public** in the same way as other Trade Register information.
* Information is only disclosed to parties that have a purpose under the money laundering act (e.g., banks, lawyers, authorities).
* **Public information:** Information on *whether* a company has made a beneficial owner notification is public and visible in the Virre information service.

## 3. Obtaining a Requested Extract
A beneficial owner extract can be ordered:
1. As a single document order from PRH's online service.
2. As a contractual customer of the Virre information service.

---
**Search:** You can check the status of the notification with the business ID in the [Virre Information Service](https://virre.prh.fi).
