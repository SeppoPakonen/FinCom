# PRH: Establishing a Limited Liability Company

A limited liability company (Oy) is formed when it is registered in the Trade Register. The establishment notification must be made electronically in the YTJ service.

## 1. Establishment Methods
* **Option A (Guided Package):** The service creates the documents. Suitable when share capital is 0 €, founders are adults and a standard articles of association is sufficient. Price: **300 €**.
* **Option B (Establishment Notification):** You add your own documents. Used when there is share capital, companies are shareholders or a custom articles of association is needed. Price: **400 €**.

## 2. What information is needed?
* Trade name (3 alternatives recommended), business activity and registered office.
* Board members and possible managing director.
* Ultimate beneficial owners (over 25% ownership or voting rights).

## 3. Points to note
* **Electronic filing:** Mandatory starting from 1.1.2026.
* **Checking the business name:** Use the [Company Name Service](https://nimipalvelu.prh.fi) to verify name availability.
* **Minor:** Can be a shareholder under certain conditions, but not a board member.
