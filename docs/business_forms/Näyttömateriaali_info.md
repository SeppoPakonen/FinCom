# Guide: Demonstration Materials for the Professional Qualification in Entrepreneurship

This document compiles information about the materials needed for the demonstration and assessment criteria.

## 1. Mandatory Parts of the Qualification
* **Working in Business Operations Tasks (30 credits):** Customer service and working in the company's daily operations.
* **Planning and Starting Business Operations (60 credits):** Business Plan, financial calculations and establishment procedures.

## 2. Elective Parts (Choose One)
* **Sales and Marketing (60 credits):** Strategy, annual calendar and budget.
* **Development of Products and Services (60 credits):** Productization and piloting.

## 3. Materials Required for the Demonstration
Ensure that the following documents are uploaded to the Moodle platform before the assessment:
* **Self-assessment forms** for each part of the qualification.
* **Business Plan (LTS)** and **Financial Calculations (YT4/YT5)**.
* **CV** and previous certificates of employment.
* **Responsibility Plan** (sustainable development).
* **Marketing Materials** (e.g., logo, brochures, social media plan).

## 4. Assessment Discussion
* **Duration:** Approximately 1 hour.
* **Participants:** Student, responsible instructor and representative of working life.
* **Decision:** Pass/Fail. The assessment is based on the qualification criteria and the presented materials.
