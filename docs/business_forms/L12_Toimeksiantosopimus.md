## 5. Resolution of Disputes

Any disputes arising from this agreement are primarily attempted to be resolved through negotiations between the parties. If no resolution is reached within 1 month of identifying the dispute, the dispute shall be resolved by an external legal mediator acceptable to both parties. If necessary, the mediator shall be selected by drawing lots between two candidates. The mediator must issue their decision within one month of selection, and the decision binds both parties without possibility of appeal.
