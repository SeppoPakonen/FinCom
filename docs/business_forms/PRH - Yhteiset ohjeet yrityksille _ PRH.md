# General Instructions for Companies Reporting to the Trade Register

From the menu, you can find instructions that are common to several or all business forms.

Also familiarize yourself with the business form-specific reporting instructions, for example, instructions concerning limited liability companies. [Familiarize yourself with the instructions for limited liability companies.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/yrityksenperustaminen/osakeyhtion-perustaminen-ja-perustamisilmoitus_1.html)

## Be careful with personal identity codes and home addresses

Do not provide a personal identity code and home address unless specifically requested. Do not attach to notifications documents that contain personal identity codes, home addresses, or sensitive information such as health information. [Familiarize yourself with the processing of personal data at PRH.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/tietoa_prhsta/tietosuoja.html)
