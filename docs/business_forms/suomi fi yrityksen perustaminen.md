# ABC of Working Life: Rules and Obligations

This guide compiles key information about employment contracts, salary matters and employee rights in Finland.

## 1. Employment Contract and Trial Period
* **Employment contract:** Agreement about work and salary. It is recommended to make it in writing.
* **Trial period:** Maximum 6 months. During the trial period, either party can terminate the contract without notice period (but not for discriminatory reasons).

## 2. Salary and Working Hours
* **Salary:** At least according to the industry's collective agreement (TES). A written salary breakdown must be provided.
* **Regular working hours:** Maximum 8 h/day and 40 h/week.
* **Overtime:** Must be agreed separately, and compensated with premium pay.

## 3. Annual Leave and Time Off
* **Annual leave:** Accumulates 2–2.5 days per month. Summer vacation (4 weeks) is taken between May and September.
* **Study leave:** Right to up to 2 years within 5 years if the employment relationship has lasted at least one year.

## 4. Ending the Employment Relationship
* **Notice period:** Depends on the length of employment (14 days – 6 months).
* **Immediate termination:** Only for very serious reasons, ends immediately.
* **Certificate of employment:** The employee has the right to receive a written certificate when the employment relationship ends.

## 5. Data Protection and Monitoring
* **GDPR:** The employer may only collect information essential for the job.
* **Monitoring:** Camera surveillance must not target individual employees or social areas. Reading emails is permitted only in exceptional circumstances.

---
**Remember:** The employer must ensure workplace safety and treat all employees fairly. Bullying must be addressed immediately.
