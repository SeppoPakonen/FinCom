# Shareholder and Partnership Agreement (Template)

This is a template for an agreement between the owners of a company. The agreement determines the relationships between the owners and their relationship to the company.

---

## Instructions and Notes
- **When to prepare:** Always when the company has multiple shareholders, new shareholders are brought in, or a shareholder changes.
- **Purpose:** To help resolve problematic situations in advance (e.g., disagreements in 50/50 ownership).
- **Connection to articles of association:** If a redemption clause is included in the agreement, it must also be mentioned in the articles of association.
- **Contract freedom:** There are no direct regulations on shareholder agreements in legislation; however, they are restricted by the law on legal acts, limited liability companies act, and employment contract act.

## 6. Rights Related to Shares

- **Pledge prohibition:** Pledging shares prohibited without permission.
- **Right of first refusal:** Consistent with the articles of association.
- **Tag-along right:** Right to sell shares at the same price if the majority is sold.
- **Drag-along obligation:** Obligation to sell shares if the majority is sold.

---

## 7. Leaving Ownership and Redemption

- **Separation:** Obligation to divest shares, e.g., due to competing business, criminal activity, or misconduct.
- **Price in disability:**      
- **Price in case of death:** Fair price (tax authority's calculation method).
- **Price when breaching agreement:** 1/3 of fair price.
- **Deadlock (50/50 situation):** Resolved by tender procedure (A offers B at price X; if B does not buy, they must sell their shares to A at the same price). The offeror is drawn by lot.
