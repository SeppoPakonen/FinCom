# Shareholder and Company Member Agreement (Template)

This is a template for an agreement between the company's owners. The agreement establishes the relationships between the owners and their relationship to the company.

---

## Instructions and Notes
- **When to prepare:** Whenever the company has multiple shareholders, new shareholders are brought in, or a shareholder changes.
- **Purpose:** To help resolve problematic situations in advance (e.g. disputes in 50/50 ownership).
- **Connection to articles of association:** If the agreement includes redemption terms, there must be a reference to this in the articles of association as well.
- **Freedom of contract:** There are no direct regulations in the legislation regarding shareholder agreements; however, they are restricted by the law on legal acts, the limited liability companies act, and the employment contract act.

## 5. Financing

## 6. Rights related to shares

- **Pledge prohibition:** Pledging shares prohibited without permission.
- **Right of first refusal:** Consistent with the articles of association.
- **Tag-along right:** Right to sell shares at the same price if the majority is sold.
- **Drag-along obligation:** Obligation to sell shares if the majority is sold.

---

## 7. Leaving ownership and redemption

- **Dismissal:** Obligation to divest shares, e.g. due to competing business, criminal activity, or misconduct.
- **Price in disability:**
- **Price in case of death:** Fair price (tax authority's calculation method).
- **Price when breaching the agreement:** 1/3 of fair market value.
- **Deadlock (50/50 situation):** Resolved through bidding procedure (A offers B price X; if B doesn't buy, B must sell their shares to A at the same price). The offer maker is drawn by lot.

- **Resolution of disputes:** Primarily negotiations. If no resolution within    months, use an external mediator (decision binding).
