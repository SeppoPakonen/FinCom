# PRH: Publishing Documents in a Language Other Than Finnish or Swedish

Trade Register notifications must always be made in Finnish or Swedish, but a limited liability company can voluntarily publish information in other languages as well.

## 1. Principles of Publication
* A notation is made in the register information about the additional languages used.
* Official translations are required according to language legislation.
* Submitting notifications on paper forms is not possible starting from 1.1.2026.

## 2. Notifications
* **Online form:** [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin)
* **New companies:** Y1 form + 1 attachment.
* **Change notification:** Y4 form + 13 attachment.
* **Attachments:** Receipt of payment, documents in a foreign language, and official translations.

## 3. Costs
* **Free:** As part of establishment or change notification.
* **55 euros:** If notified as a separate matter on the online form.

## 4. Foreign Notifiers (no Finnish SSN)
An EU country citizen can notify electronically if:
1. They have an identification tool from another EU country.
2. The notification concerns a limited liability company or branch.
