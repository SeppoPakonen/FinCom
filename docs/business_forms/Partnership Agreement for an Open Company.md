# Partnership Agreement for an Open Company

This is a template for a partnership agreement for an open company. When filling out the agreement, the particularities of each company must be taken into account.

---

## Instructions
- Fill in the required sections.
- Remember signatures after printing.
- A shareholder/partnership agreement should always be prepared when the company has more than one responsible partner.
- **Disclaimer:** The template is an example, and the author is not liable for its suitability or correctness in all individual cases.
- **Personal Data Form:** Shareholders' personal information (social security number, address) is reported on a separate form due to the Trade Register's data protection practices.

### 6. Chief Executive Officer
- A chief executive officer may be appointed to take care of the company's ongoing administration.

### 7. Profit Distribution
- The company's profit is shared equally among the partners unless otherwise agreed.

### 8. Death of a Partner
- The company does not need to be dissolved upon a partner's death.
- The heirs take the place of the deceased upon reaching 18 years of age.
- Willingness to become a partner must be announced within three months of reaching majority or death.
- Redemption price: If the heir does not wish to become a partner, the company's value is calculated as the difference between assets and liabilities reduced by 30 percent (valuation with the help of experts).

### 9. Dissolution of the Company
- Valid until further notice.
- Notice period is three months.
- If one partner continues operations, they must redeem the departing party's share according to section 8.
