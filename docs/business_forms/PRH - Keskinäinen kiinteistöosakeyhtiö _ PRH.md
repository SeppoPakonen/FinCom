# PRH: Mutual Real Estate Company

A mutual real estate company (koy) is a company form where shares provide the right to occupy a specific space or unit in the company's building.

## 1. Applicable Law
* Primarily, the **Apartment Ownership Act** applies.
* The articles of association may specify that the Limited Liability Companies Act applies (either completely or partially).

## 2. Establishment
* **Deadline:** The notification must be made within **3 months** of signing the articles of incorporation.
* **Costs:** The establishment notification costs 400 â‚¬.
* **Unit Information System:** New companies automatically belong to the system maintained by the National Land Survey of Finland.

## 3. Required Information and Attachments
* Original articles of incorporation.
* Board members and managing director/facility manager's certificate of compliance with the law.
* Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
* **Articles of Association:** Must be in electronic (structured) format. Includes, among other things, the unit description (Section 4).

## 4. Notification
The notification is made electronically in the [YTJ service](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap). The Business ID is received immediately after submission.
