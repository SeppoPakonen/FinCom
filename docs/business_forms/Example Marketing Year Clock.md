# Example: Marketing Year Clock (Tauno Renovation Contractor)

This is an example of a small business owner's (Tauno Renovation Contractor) annual marketing plan.

---

## Continuous Activities (Throughout the Year)
- **Social media accounts:** Updates twice a week.
- **Customer register:** Continuous maintenance and updates.

---

## Monthly Activities

### January (Establishment)
- Setting up websites.
- Creating Facebook and Instagram accounts.
- Printing work clothes and car decals.

### February (Visibility)
- Mapping potential market areas.
- Designing printable A5 flyers.
- Distributing flyers to letterboxes.

### March
- Paid social media marketing (1 time per week).
- Door-to-door rounds.

### April
- Paid social media marketing (2 times per week).
- Establishing customer register (collecting contacts and marketing permissions).

### May
- Maintaining customer information in registers.

### June
- Take 1 week of vacation.

### July
- Take 2 weeks of vacation.

### August
- Social media marketing (1 time per week).
- Distributing flyers to letterboxes.

### September (Active Sales)
- Paid social media marketing (2 times per week).
- Sending offers to existing customers via email and text message.
- Door-to-door rounds.

### October
- Paid social media marketing (1 time per week).
- Sending offer letters to apartment buildings.

### November
- Paid social media marketing (1 time per week).
- Follow-up: surveying apartment buildings about offers.

### December
- Sending Christmas cards to existing customers.
- Take 1 week of vacation.
