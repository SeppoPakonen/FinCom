# PRH: Mutual Real Estate Company

A mutual real estate company (koy) is a company form in which shares provide the right to manage a specific space or apartment in the company's building.

## 1. Applicable law
* As a rule, the **housing company act** is applied.
* The articles of association may provide that the limited liability companies act is applied (either completely or partially).

## 2. Establishment
* **Deadline:** The notification must be made **3 months** after signing the articles of incorporation.
* **Costs:** The establishment notification costs 400 â‚¬.
* **Apartment information system:** New companies automatically belong to the system maintained by the National Land Survey.

## 3. Required information and attachments
* Original articles of incorporation.
* Board and managing director/property manager's guarantee of compliance with the law.
* Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
* **Articles of association:** Must be in electronic (structured) form. Includes, among others, apartment specifications (Section 4).

## 4. Notification
The notification is made electronically in the [YTJ service](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap). The business ID is received immediately after submission.
