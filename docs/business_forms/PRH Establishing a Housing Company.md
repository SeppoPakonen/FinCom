# PRH: Establishing a Housing Company

A housing company (as-oy) is formed by registering it in the Trade Register. New companies are automatically included in the apartment information system.

## 1. Timeline and Responsible Personnel
* **Deadline:** The establishment notification must be made **3 months** after signing the articles of association. Delaying results in the establishment lapsing.
* **Signatory:** One of the board members.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 400 €.
* **Auxiliary trade name:** 75 € / name.

## 3. Required Attachments
1. Original, signed articles of association.
2. Board and property manager's guarantee of compliance with the law.
3. Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
4. Possible attachments to the articles of association (e.g., liability distribution table).
5. Copies of identification documents for persons who do not have a Finnish social security number.

## 4. Notification (YTJ service)
* Notification is made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* The articles of association are provided in electronic form. Sections 1–4 are standardized and the service creates them based on the provided information.
* The list of shareholders is transmitted directly from the service to the National Land Survey.

## 5. Monitoring
* The business ID is received immediately after submission.
* Processing can be monitored in the [Virre Information Service](https://virre.prh.fi).

---
**Note!** Do not attach documents containing sensitive information (e.g., health information) or home addresses unless specifically requested.
