# PRH: General Partnership (Ay) and Limited Partnership (Ky)

A notification of establishment must always be made for a general partnership and limited partnership. The company is formed by registration.

## 1. Establishment Process
* **Deadline:** The notification must be submitted within **3 months** of signing the partnership agreement.
* **Signing:**
    - **Ay:** One of the partners.
    - **Ky:** One of the general partners (responsible partners).
    - Procuration does not entitle to sign the notification.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 300 €.
* **Auxiliary business name:** 75 € / name.
* The fee must be paid before submitting the notification.

## 3. Required Documents
* **Form Y2** and separate **personal information form** (containing personal identity codes and addresses).
* Original partnership agreement.
* Statement on the appointment of managing director or procurator (if appointed).
* Receipt of the processing fee.

## 4. Minor as a Partner
A person under 18 years of age can be a partner if:
1. Guardians represent them.
2. Guardians have permission from the Digital and Population Data Services Agency (**DVV**) for the action.

## 5. Notification of Financial Year
The financial year must be notified to the Trade Register if the general partner is a limited liability company or another general partnership/limited partnership whose general partner is a limited liability company.
