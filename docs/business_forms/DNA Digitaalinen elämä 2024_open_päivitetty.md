# DNA Digital Life 2024 - Research Report

This document is a condensed summary of a study conducted by DNA in 2024, describing Finnish people's digital lifestyle habits, device usage and attitudes.

---

## 1. Background and Methodology of the Study

- **Objective:** To understand and describe Finnish people's digital lifestyle habits (research series started in 2017).
- **Target group:** All Finns over 15 years old (upper age limit removed in 2024).
- **Data collection:** March 18–27, 2024 from Nepa's online panels.
- **Sample size:** 1018 people (weighted to represent the population by age and gender).

---

## 2. Smartphone Usage and Prevalence

### Phones
- **Smartphone ownership:** 98 % of Finns own a smartphone.
- **5G technology:** 56 % have a 5G phone in use (significant growth from 2021, when the share was 17 %).
- **Age differences:** 5G usage is emphasized among those under 55, but smartphones are common in all age groups (min. 95 %).

### Other devices in households (Top 5)
1. Laptop computer: 82 %
2. Smart TV: 61 %
3. Tablet computer: 58 %
4. Desktop computer: 46 %
5. Gaming console: 42 %

**Trends:** Smart TV and smartwatch usage is becoming more common, while traditional TVs and recording digital boxes are decreasing in use.

---

## 3. Apps and Services Used

App usage has increased in almost all areas.

### Most popular app types (2024):
- **Banking services:** 83 %
- **Communication (e.g. WhatsApp):** 77 %
- **Maps and routes:** 77 %
- **Social media:** 75 %
- **Mobile payments (e.g. MobilePay):** 65 % (+7 %-points growth from previous year)
- **Shopping cart apps:** 53 % (+17 %-points significant growth)

### Age-group differences in apps:
- **Under 25 years:** Entertainment is emphasized (music, streaming, Snapchat).
- **Older age groups:** Utility apps are emphasized (banks, maps, security).
- **25–34 years:** Use all app types evenly, especially mobile payments.

---

## 4. Social Media Usage

### Daily usage (Top 5):
1. **WhatsApp:** 70 % (most active in all age groups)
2. **Facebook:** 61 %
3. **Instagram:** 40 %
4. **YouTube (free version):** 38 %
5. **Snapchat:** 25 %

**Notes:** Instagram and especially TikTok's popularity is increasing. Half of Finns use WhatsApp numerous times per day.

---

## 5. Future Outlook and Artificial Intelligence

- **Artificial intelligence:** 49 % believe they will use AI (e.g. ChatGPT) regularly or occasionally within the next two years. Among under 25-year-olds, the share is 90 %.
- **Mobile payments:** Usage is believed to continue growing (78 % believe they will use it in the future).

---

## 6. Attitudes towards Digitalization

- **Benefits:** Digital services are perceived as meaningful and people want to handle matters online.
- **Challenges:**
    - 39 % feel digital services interfere with concentration.
    - 38 % find it difficult to disconnect from the internet.
    - 31 % are interested in buying used smartphones.
- **Change:** Attitudes towards adopting new services quickly have slightly cooled compared to the previous year.
