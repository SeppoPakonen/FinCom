# Company Formation Requirements in Finland

## Overview
This document outlines the requirements for forming a limited liability company (Oy) in Finland.

## Required Documents
- Articles of Association
- Proof of minimum share capital (€2,500)
- Identity verification for all shareholders
- Registered office address confirmation

## Registration Process
1. Prepare Articles of Association
2. Deposit minimum share capital
3. Submit registration to Patent and Registration Office (PRH)
4. Await confirmation (typically 2-3 weeks)

## Important Constraints
- Minimum share capital of €2,500 must be deposited before registration
- At least one shareholder is required
- A Finnish registered office address is mandatory
- The company must have at least one director who is a resident of Finland

## Tax Obligations
New companies must register for VAT if annual turnover exceeds €10,000.
Corporate tax rate is 20% of profits.

## Compliance Requirements
- Annual financial statements must be filed
- Board members have fiduciary duties
- Shareholders have voting rights proportional to shares held
