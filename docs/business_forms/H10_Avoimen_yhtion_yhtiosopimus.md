### 6. Managing Director
- The company may appoint a managing director to take care of the company's ongoing administration.

### 7. Profit sharing
- The company's profit is shared equally among the partners unless otherwise agreed.

### 8. Death of a partner
- The partnership does not need to be dissolved when a partner dies.
- The legal heirs take the place of the deceased upon reaching 18 years of age.
- The willingness to become a partner must be declared within 3 months of reaching adulthood or from the time of death.
- Redemption price: If the heir does not wish to become a partner, the company's value is calculated as the difference between assets and liabilities reduced by 30 percent (valuation with the help of experts).

### 9. Dissolution of the partnership
- Valid until further notice.
- The notice period is 3 months.
- If one partner continues the business, they must redeem the departing party's share according to point 8.

### 10. Other provisions
- Otherwise, the Act on General Partnership and Limited Partnership is applied.
