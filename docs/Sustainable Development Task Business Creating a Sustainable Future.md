# Sustainable Development Task: Business Creating a Sustainable Future

This is a complementary task for the qualification unit **Planning and Starting Business Operations**. The task is attached to the assessment materials.

---

## Task Instructions

Answer the following questions using lecture materials and considering various aspects of responsibility:

### a) Business Idea
- Briefly describe your company's business idea.

### b) Industry Responsibility
- What are the key ecological, ethical, and social responsibility aspects in your industry?

### c) Future Outlook
- Consider how sustainable development will appear in your industry in the future.
- How do you think customer needs will change with this development?

### d) Entrepreneur's Influence Opportunities
- How can you as an entrepreneur influence the mitigation of climate change, environmental issues, and overconsumption of natural resources?

### e) Practical Implementation
- How do you concretely implement sustainable development in your own business operations and as an entrepreneur?

---

**Professional Skill Requirement:**  
The student can consider the principles of sustainable development in planning and starting business operations.
