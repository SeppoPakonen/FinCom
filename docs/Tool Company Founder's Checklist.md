# Tool: Company Founder's Checklist

This checklist helps you ensure that all important matters are handled during the early stages of your company.

## 1. Planning and Preparation
* [ ] **Clarifying the business idea:** What do you sell, to whom, and how?
* [ ] **BP and calculations:** Prepare a business plan and financial calculations (YT4/YT5).
* [ ] **Expert assistance:** Book time for business consulting (e.g., New Business Center).
* [ ] **Startup grant:** Apply for a decision BEFORE registering the company.

## 2. Official Registrations
* [ ] **Establishment notification:** Make the notification in the [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi) service.
* [ ] **Tax registration:** Apply to the advance tax collection and VAT registers.
* [ ] **Trademark:** Protect the company name or logo if necessary at PRH.

## 3. Banking and Insurance
* [ ] **Business account:** Open a bank account and obtain a payment terminal or online store payments.
* [ ] **YEL insurance:** Take out mandatory pension insurance from a pension company.
* [ ] **Other insurance:** Liability insurance, accident insurance, and legal protection insurance.

## 4. Everyday Tools
* [ ] **Bookkeeping:** Choose an accounting firm and arrange electronic financial management.
* [ ] **Contract templates:** Prepare templates for offers, orders, and invoices.
* [ ] **IT equipment:** Computer, phone, and necessary software.

## 5. Sales and Marketing
* [ ] **Online visibility:** Reserve a domain, create websites, and open social media channels.
* [ ] **Logo design:** Create a recognizable image for your company.
* [ ] **Pricing:** Decide on the prices of products and services.

---
**Remember:** Networking is important! Join a local entrepreneur organization or professional network.
