# Checklist: Demonstration and Networking

This is a summary of important dates and considerations related to the demonstration for the professional qualification in entrepreneurship.

## Important Deadlines
* **11.1.2026:** Self-assessment form (Word template) must be returned to Moodle at the latest.
* **18.2.2026 at 13:30–14:30:** Entrepreneur demonstration and interview.
    - Target: Only the first mandatory part of the qualification.

## Demonstration Materials
* **Self-Assessment Form:** The discussion in the demonstration is based on this form.
* **CV:** Previously submitted, but can and should be updated if necessary.

## Networking in Oulu
Utilize local organizations for customer acquisition and support:
* **North Ostrobothnia Entrepreneurs (Pohjois-Pohjanmaan Yrittäjät)**
* **Myry ry:** Small business advocacy organization.
* **Facebook Groups:** The *Oulun Yrittäjät* group is a good place to network.
* **Social Media Strategy:** TikTok is good for marketing, but professional networking should be done through other channels or association activities.

## Business Plan Supplement
Ensure that the business plan justifies the following:
1. Background and work experience of the owners.
2. Comprehensive competitor analysis.
3. Clear business idea.
