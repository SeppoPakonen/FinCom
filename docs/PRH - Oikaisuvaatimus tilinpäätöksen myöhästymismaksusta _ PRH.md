# PRH: Request for Correction of Late Fee

If a company has received a decision on a late fee for a financial statement, it can request PRH to correct the decision.

## 1. Grounds that are NOT accepted
PRH generally does not correct the fee if the grounds are:
* Insolvent company or lack of funds.
* Unawareness of reporting obligation.
* Bookkeeper's forgotten notification.

## 2. How to make a correction request
* **Deadline:** 30 days from receipt of the decision.
* **Method:** Electronically via web form at [turvaviesti.prh.fi](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/oikaisuvaatimus-myohastymismaksu) or by mail.
* **Fee:** Free of charge.

## 3. Required information
* Company name and Business ID.
* Case number of the decision.
* Written justification for why the fee was imposed without grounds.

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 36.
