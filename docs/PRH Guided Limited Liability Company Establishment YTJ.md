# PRH: Guided Limited Liability Company Establishment (YTJ)

You can easily establish a limited liability company with an electronic establishment package if the company's information is in standard format.

## 1. When can you use the guided package?
* Share capital is **0 euros**.
* All founders are adults with Finnish personal identity codes.
* A standard articles of association is sufficient.
* All responsible persons have electronic identification tools (e.g., online banking credentials).

## 2. Notification Steps
1. **Log in:** [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
2. **Information:** Enter name (3 options), business activity, financial year, and registered office.
3. **Persons:** Report the board, beneficial owners, and possible managing director.
4. **Signature:** All shareholders and board members must visit the service to sign the notification.

## 3. Timeline
* The guided package must be completed within **60 days** of starting, otherwise the establishment lapses.
* The business ID is received immediately after sending the notification.

**Note:** If any responsible person does not have a Finnish SSN, the traditional establishment notification must be used (documents attached as PDF files).
