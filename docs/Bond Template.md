The creditor has the right to immediately cancel the debt for repayment in the following situations:
- The debtor delays payment of an installment or interest and does not rectify the negligence within 14 days of notice.
- The value-share account serving as collateral for the debt is converted to cash.
- The debtor is petitioned for bankruptcy or corporate restructuring (or petitions for them themselves).
