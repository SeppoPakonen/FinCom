# Checklist: Establishing a Company (Business Plan)

This checklist helps ensure that all critical aspects of the business plan and establishment have been considered.

## 1. Business Idea and Goals
- [ ] Is the business idea clearly articulated?
- [ ] Has a vision been set for 5–10 years ahead?
- [ ] Are the first year's goals measurable?

## 2. Markets and Competition
- [ ] Has the main target group been defined?
- [ ] Has competitor analysis been done (at least 2–3 competitors)?
- [ ] What is the company's unique competitive advantage (USP)?

## 3. Finances and Funding
- [ ] Has a financial plan been made (where is the money needed)?
- [ ] Have profitability calculations and cash budget been prepared?
- [ ] Has the startup grant application been submitted (if applying)?

## 4. Practical Establishment
- [ ] Has the company form been chosen?
- [ ] Has the company name been checked and protected?
- [ ] Has the establishment notification been submitted (YTJ)?
- [ ] Are the mandatory insurances (e.g. YEL) in order?
