# Guarantee of Compliance with the Law (Establishing Housing Companies and Mutual Real Estate Companies)

When establishing a housing company or mutual real estate company, you provide a guarantee of compliance with the law by the board members and property manager or managing director.

This page contains an example of a guarantee that you attach to the establishment notification of a housing company or mutual real estate company.

## What information must the guarantee contain?

The guarantee must contain the following:

* Who provides the guarantee (in addition to board members, either the property manager or managing director)
* Which law was followed in the establishment (the Housing Company Act or the Limited Liability Companies Act).
