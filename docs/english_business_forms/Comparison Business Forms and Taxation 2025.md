# Comparison: Business Forms and Taxation 2025

The choice of company form affects your responsibilities, decision-making, profit distribution, and taxation.

## 1. Private Entrepreneur (Trade Name)
* **Suitable for:** Solo entrepreneurs, small-scale operations.
* **Liability:** Full personal liability with all assets.
* **Taxation:** The profit is taxed as the entrepreneur's personal earned and capital income.
* **Establishment:** Easy and affordable (approximately 70 €).

## 2. Partnerships (Ay and Ky)
* **Open Company (Ay):** At least two partners. All have unlimited and joint liability. Requires strong trust.
* **Limited Partnership (Ky):** At least one general partner (liable with all assets) and one silent partner (investor, liability only with the invested amount).
* **Taxation:** The profit is distributed among the partners for taxation according to the partnership agreement.

## 3. Limited Liability Company (Oy)
* **Suitable for:** Companies of all sizes, professional growth.
* **Liability:** The company itself is liable for its debts. The shareholder's risk is limited to the invested capital.
* **Decision-making:** General meeting and board of directors. Share capital is not mandatory (0 €).
* **Taxation:** Independent taxpayer (corporate tax 20%). Funds to owners as salary or dividends.

## 4. Light Entrepreneurship
* Not an actual business form, but a way to invoice for work without having your own Business ID (through a service provider). A good way to test a business idea.

---
**Tip:** Choose the form that best protects you from the risks of operations. A limited liability company is often the safest choice for growing business operations.
