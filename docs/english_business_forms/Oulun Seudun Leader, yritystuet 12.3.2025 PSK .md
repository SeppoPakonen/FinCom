# Business Support: Oulu Region Leader (2023–2027)

Oulu Region Leader provides funding and consulting for different phases of business operations in six municipalities in North Ostrobothnia.

## 1. Operating Area and Requirements
* **Area:** Operates in rural areas. Note: The city center of Oulu is not included in the operating area.
* **Profitability:** The applicant must have the prerequisites for profitable operations and sufficient financial resources to implement the project.
* **Business Plan:** An up-to-date business plan must be attached to the application.

## 2. Support for Experimentation and Planning

### Testing Business Operations (2,500 €)
* **Target:** For individuals who do not yet have a Business ID.
* **Purpose:** To investigate the market potential of an idea and personal readiness for entrepreneurship.
* **Duration:** Max 4 months.

### Planning Business Operations (1,000 €)
* **Target:** For those planning business operations.
* **Purpose:** Investigating the starting situation and developing the business idea.

## 3. Startup Support (Business ID required)

### Part-time Entrepreneurship (5,000 €)
* **Requirement:** Working time 10–35 h/week, targeted revenue at least 10,000 €.

### Full-time Entrepreneurship (7,500 €)
* **Requirement:** Working time at least 35 h/week, targeted revenue at least 15,000 €.

### Preparing for Ownership Change (2,500 – 5,000 €)
* **Target:** For planning the purchase of a company located in rural areas (not for the purchase price itself).

## 4. Investment Support and Development Assistance

### Development Assistance (3,000 €)
* **Target:** For established micro-enterprises for mentoring and expert services.

### Investment Support (30–40 %)
* **Target:** For investments of 5,000 – 100,000 € (machines, equipment, buildings, software, patents).

## 5. Youth Leader (100 – 500 €)
* **Target:** Young people aged 13–25 for their own projects, events and entrepreneurship experiments.
* **Support:** 90–100 % of costs.
