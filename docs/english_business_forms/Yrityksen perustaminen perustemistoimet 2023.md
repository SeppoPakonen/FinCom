# Tool: Company Founder's Checklist

This checklist helps you ensure that all important matters are handled during the early stages of your company.

## 1. Planning and Preparation
* [ ] **Clarify your business idea:** What do you sell, to whom and how?
* [ ] **Business plan and calculations:** Create a business plan and financial calculations (YT4/YT5).
* [ ] **Expert help:** Book time for business consulting (e.g. New Business Center).
* [ ] **Startup grant:** Apply for a decision BEFORE company registration.

## 2. Official Registrations
* [ ] **Establishment notification:** Submit notification in the [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi) service.
* [ ] **Tax registration:** Apply for advance payment and VAT registers.
* [ ] **Trademark:** Protect your company name or logo if needed at PRH.

## 3. Bank and Insurance
* [ ] **Business account:** Open a bank account and get a payment terminal or e-commerce payments.
* [ ] **YEL insurance:** Take out mandatory pension insurance from a pension company.
* [ ] **Other insurance:** Liability insurance, accident insurance and legal protection insurance.

## 4. Everyday Tools
* [ ] **Accounting:** Choose an accounting firm and arrange electronic financial management.
* [ ] **Contract templates:** Prepare templates for offers, orders and invoices.
* [ ] **IT equipment:** Computer, phone and necessary software.

## 5. Sales and Marketing
* [ ] **Online visibility:** Reserve a domain, create a website and open social media channels.
* [ ] **Logo design:** Create a recognizable image for your company.
* [ ] **Pricing:** Determine prices for products and services.

---
**Remember:** Networking is important! Join a local entrepreneur organization or professional network.
