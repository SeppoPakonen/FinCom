# Guide: Business Law and Permits

Many laws and government regulations govern business operations. This is a summary of the most important areas.

## 1. Choosing a Business Form
Legislation sets different requirements for different business forms:
* **Business Name (Tmi):** Simplest option, entrepreneur personally liable for everything.
* **Limited Liability Company (Oy):** Requires at least one member and alternate member for the board. Shareholders' liability is limited to the invested capital.
* **Partnerships (Ay, Ky):** Based on agreement and liability of the partners.

## 2. Licensed Business Activities
Certain industries require a separate license or notification before starting:
* **Food industry:** Notification to the municipality and self-monitoring plan.
* **Beauty care:** Notification according to the Health Protection Act.
* **Alcohol:** Serving and retail sales permits (AVI).
* **Transportation:** Traffic permit.

## 3. Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)
* **Copyright:** Automatically protects creative work (e.g. music, text, images). Valid for 70 years after the author's death.
* **Trademark:** Protects the company's name or logo. Registered with PRH.
* **Patent:** Protects a technical invention.

## 4. Data Protection (GDPR)
All companies that process personal data (e.g. customer register) must comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation.
* **Important:** Prepare a privacy policy and obtain consent for marketing.

## 5. Consumer Protection
Consumer sales must comply with the Consumer Protection Act, which regulates, among other things, liability for defects, right of withdrawal in distance selling (online store), and truthfulness of marketing.
