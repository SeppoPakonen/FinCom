## 5. Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising from this agreement are primarily attempted to be resolved through negotiations between the parties. If no resolution is reached within 1 month of identifying the dispute, the matter is resolved by an external legal mediator acceptable to both parties. If necessary, the mediator is selected by lot between two candidates. The mediator must provide their decision within one month of selection, and the decision binds both parties without possibility of appeal.
