# PRH: Ltd Establishment Notification (Own Documents)

Use this notification method if the limited liability company's information does not suit the guided formation package.

## 1. When do you choose this option?
* The company has **share capital**.
* Companies or other entities are shareholders.
* One of the responsible persons does not have a Finnish personal identity code.
* A standard articles of association is not sufficient.
* The company is a public limited liability company (Ltd plc).

## 3. Required Attachments
* Self-prepared articles of incorporation.
* Board members' and managing director's declaration of compliance with the law.
* Auditor's certificate or receipt of payment of shares.
* Possible **contribution in kind statement** and auditor's statement on it.

## 4. Notification
* Made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* Write the complete articles of association in the service's text field.
* Report responsible persons and ultimate beneficial owners at the same time.
