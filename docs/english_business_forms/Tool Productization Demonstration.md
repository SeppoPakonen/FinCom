# Tool: Productization Demonstration

Use this form to document the development process of a new service or product.

## 1. Business Idea and Need
* **What is the product/service?** Brief description.
* **What problem does it solve?**
* **Who is it intended for?** (Target customer group)

## 2. Brand and Image
* **What brand are you building?** What impressions do you want to create?
* **Competitive advantage:** Why would the customer buy from you?

## 3. Implementation and Piloting
* **How was the product tested?** (Customer surveys, pilots, interviews).
* **Received feedback:** What did you learn from the testing?
* **Changes:** How did you develop the product based on the feedback?

## 4. Economics and Legislation
* **Pricing:** How is the price defined? (Attach pricing calculations).
* **Sales target:** How much is intended to be sold?
* **Contracts:** What protections (trademark) or agreements (NDA) are needed?

## 5. Responsibility and Digitalization
* **Sustainability:** How has responsibility been considered in the product's lifecycle?
* **Digitalization:** How do you utilize digital tools in sales or production?

---
**Attachments:** Product cards, visual materials, pricing calculations.
