# PRH: Filing Financial Statements

Submitting financial statements to the Trade Register is a legal obligation for most business forms, even if there was no actual business activity during the year.

## 1. Who is subject to the reporting obligation?
* **Always:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives and foreign branches.
* **Under certain conditions:** General partnerships, limited partnerships and private entrepreneurs (if thresholds are exceeded).
* **Not applicable:** Housing companies and real estate companies.

## 2. Timeline
* **Limited liability companies and Cooperatives:** Within 8 months of the end of the financial year. (Must first be approved at the general meeting).
* **Branch offices and others:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.

## 3. Costs
* **Free:** When the notification is submitted on time in the YTJ service.
* **Fee-based:** If the notification is late or submitted on paper form (100 €).

## 4. Consequences of delay
* Late filing fee (150–600 €).
* In the worst case, removal of the company from the Trade Register.
