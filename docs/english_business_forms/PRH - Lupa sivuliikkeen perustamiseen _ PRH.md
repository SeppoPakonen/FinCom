# PRH: Permit for Establishing a Branch Office

If the headquarters of a foreign company is located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), it needs a permit from PRH to establish a branch office in Finland.

## 1. Granting the Permit
The permit is usually granted if the head office is properly registered in its home country.

## 2. Branch Office Representative
* The branch office must have an **representative** who has the right to receive service of process on behalf of the company.
* If the head office is from the EEA, the representative's residence must be in the EEA.
* If the head office is from outside the EEA, the representative's registered office must be **in Finland**.

## 3. Applying
* **Method:** Online form at [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin).
* **Fee:** 150 € (+ 6.50 € billing fee).
* **Attachments:**
    - Free-form application.
    - Registry extract from the head office.
    - Copy of articles of association.
    - Official translations to Finnish or Swedish.

## 4. Next Steps
After receiving the permit, a proper **branch office establishment notification** must be made to the Trade Register, attaching the permit decision.
