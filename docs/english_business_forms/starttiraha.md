# Information about the Startup Grant

The startup grant is a personal support for new entrepreneurs to secure their livelihood during the initial phase of business operations.

## 1. Requirements for Approval
* **Full-time activity:** Entrepreneurship must be the primary occupation.
* **Qualifications:** The applicant must have sufficient expertise or education for the field.
* **Necessity:** The support must be necessary for the entrepreneur's livelihood (not granted if the entrepreneur already receives other income, e.g. salary).
* **Profitability:** The company must have the prerequisites for continuous profitable operations.
* **Competition situation:** The support must not distort local competition.

## 2. Amount and Duration of Support
* **Amount:** Equivalent to the basic daily allowance (37.21 €/day in 2024–2026).
* **Payment:** Paid for at most five days per calendar week.
* **Duration:** Usually 6 months at a time, maximum 12 months.
* **Taxation:** The startup grant is taxable income for the entrepreneur.

## 3. Application
* **When:** The application must be submitted **before** starting business operations.
* **Where:** Applied through the Labor Market Office (TE services or municipal trial).
* **Attachments:** Business plan, profitability calculations and tax debt certificate.

---
**Important:** Do not register the company or start operations before receiving an approval decision for the startup grant!
