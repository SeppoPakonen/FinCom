Make a change notification to the Trade Register immediately when there are changes to the company's registered information.

We recommend first checking the company's current registered information from the Trade Register extract. This way you can see what information needs to be changed. Download a free Trade Register extract by searching for company information in the [Virre information service. Opens in a new tab](https://virre.prh.fi/novus/home?userLang=en)​

## Report electronically: see instructions in the menu

Starting from 2026, companies must make notifications and applications to the Trade Register electronically. This change does not concern trade name entrepreneurs (private entrepreneurs) or associations and foundations. Read more about [mandatory electronic service.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/mandatory-electronic-trade-register-service.html)

Make the notification in the [YTJ service Opens in a new tab](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap) if possible. In the YTJ service, you can report the most common changes for the most common business forms to the Trade Register. See [notifications made in the YTJ service from the ytj.fi website. Opens in a new tab](https://www.ytj.fi/en/notification/notification-in-ytj-service/business-forms-in-ytj-service.html)

If you cannot report in the YTJ service, make the notification with one of the [Trade Register's online forms.](https://www.prh.fi/en/forms_1/forms_6.html#how-do-you-notify-with-an-online-form)​

You can find the instructions for notification from the menu by business form.

## Update beneficial owner information

Make a notification when there are changes in the company's ownership or control. [Read more in the instructions: Beneficial owner notification to the Trade Register.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/beneficial-owner-information.html)​

## Annual accounts to the Trade Register after the financial year ends

Most companies must report their annual accounts to the Trade Register after their financial year has ended. [Read the instructions: Annual Accounts to the Trade Register.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/annualaccounts.html)​

## How do you track the processing of your notification?

Review the [Trade Register's processing times.](https://www.prh.fi/en/processing-times/processing-times.html)​

You can track the status of the notification you sent to PRH in the [Virre information service notification search. Opens in a new tab](https://virre.prh.fi/novus/tradeNoticeSearch?userLang=en)​
