# Tool: Safety Document (Services Containing Risks)

This is a model for a safety document according to the Consumer Safety Act (920/2011) as per Tukes' guidelines. It is primarily a company's own tool for continuous improvement of safety.

---

## 1. Basic Information
* **Service Provider:** [Company name]
* **Services:** [Which services does this document concern?]
* **Location:** [Where is the service implemented?]
* **Updated:** [Date]

## 2. Responsible Personnel
* Who is responsible for the service's safety? Name the responsible person and the on-site responsible person (if different).
* Who handles practical safety matters?

## 3. Hazard Identification
* What are the specific hazards and risks of the service (from the customer's perspective)?
* Assess all stages of the service. Also identify "potential" hazards that have not yet occurred.

## 4. Measures to Prevent Dangerous Situations
* How have hazards been prevented so far?
* What are the procedures for accidents or near-miss situations?
* What improvements are planned for the future?

## 5. Dynamic Risk Management
* How is the daily safety situation monitored (e.g., weather changes, unexpected customer behavior)?
* How are changes responded to in practice?

## 6. Staff and Customer Considerations
* **Staff:** Required professional skills, first aid skills, and orientation.
* **Customers:** How are they oriented and how are they supervised during the service? Ensuring understanding of instructions.
* **Limitations:** Environmental conditions (when the service cannot be performed safely) and participation limitations (e.g., age, height).

## 7. Environment and Equipment
* How is the safe condition of premises, equipment, and devices ensured?
* Maintenance and inspection plan.

## 8. Accident Documentation and Authority Notifications
* How are near-miss situations documented and investigated?
* **Notification to Tukes:** Serious accidents must be reported immediately to Tukes (kirjaamo@tukes.fi).

---
**Attachments:** Risk assessment forms, training register, maintenance records, accident register.
