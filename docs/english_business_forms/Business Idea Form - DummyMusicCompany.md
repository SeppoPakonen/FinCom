# Business Idea Form - DummyMusicCompany

## Business Idea

A multi-faceted music services company that primarily creates custom songs to order (personal songs and advertising jingles/songs), along with music production and recording services using a portable setup, distribution management via DistroKid, music production education through private lessons (€30-50 per 75 minutes), live online sessions for artists, and assistance with setting up online merchandise services.

## Customer Needs

Individuals wanting personalized songs for special occasions, businesses needing custom jingles or advertising music, artists who require professional music production services but don't have access to a studio, musicians needing help with music distribution, those who want to learn music production skills, and artists requiring assistance with the technical aspects of releasing and selling their music. The business addresses the need for accessible, flexible, and affordable music services that help independent artists create, distribute, and monetize their music without major upfront investment.

## Brand/Reputation Strategy

Positioning as a versatile, accessible music service provider that combines creativity (custom songwriting) with technical expertise (production, distribution, education). The brand focuses on being the go-to professional for personalized music solutions at affordable rates, bridging the gap between creative vision and technical execution.

## Target Customer Groups

1. Individuals seeking personalized songs for special occasions
2. Small businesses and advertisers needing custom jingles or branded audio content
3. Independent musicians/artists requiring production, recording, or distribution services
4. Music production students wanting to learn music production techniques
5. Content creators needing custom music for their content

## Products and Services Offered

1. **Custom Song Creation** - Personalized songs to order for individuals and businesses
2. **Music Production & Recording Services** using portable equipment
3. **Music Distribution Management** through DistroKid
4. **Music Production Education** through private lessons
5. **Live Online Music Sessions** for artists
6. **Music Merchandise Setup Assistance**

## Operational Strategy

Using a flexible, mobile-first approach allowing direct engagement with customers while maintaining low overhead costs. The model includes consultation and custom creation process, portable production services, digital distribution management, and personalized education sessions. Operations leverage cloud-based tools, video conferencing, and various music platforms to deliver services efficiently.

## Physical Resources

Portable music production equipment including DAW software, audio interface, microphones, monitors, MIDI controller, and related accessories. The mobile setup allows operations at various locations, reducing fixed costs while maintaining service quality.

## Financial Resources

Low-cost operation using existing equipment. Operating expenses include local transportation (€50/month), website hosting (€10/month), and minimal overhead. Revenue from custom songs (€50-200), production services (€25-75/hour), lessons (€30-50/75min), and distribution management (€10-50/month per artist). No initial registration or marketing costs required.

## Mental Resources

Strong technical skills in music production, composition, and audio engineering. Teaching abilities for educational services. Personal attributes include creativity, adaptability, organizational skills, and the ability to work independently. Focus on building new professional relationships and networks as the business grows.
