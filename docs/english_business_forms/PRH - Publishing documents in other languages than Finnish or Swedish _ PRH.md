# PRH: Publishing Documents in Other Languages

Trade Register notifications must always be made in Finnish or Swedish, but a limited liability company may voluntarily publish information in other languages as well.

## 1. Principles of Publication
* A notation will be made in the register information about the additional languages used.
* Official translations according to the Language Act are required.
* Notification using paper forms is not possible starting from 1.1.2026.

## 2. Notification
* **Online form:** [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin)
* **New companies:** Form Y1 + Attachment 1.
* **Change notification:** Form Y4 + Attachment 13.
* **Attachments:** Receipt of payment, documents in a foreign language and official translations.

## 3. Costs
* **Free:** As part of establishment or change notification.
* **55 euros:** If notified as a separate matter using the online form.

## 4. Foreign Notifiers (without Finnish SSN)
An EU citizen may notify electronically if:
1. They have an identification tool from another EU country.
2. The notification concerns a limited liability company or branch office.

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 19 and Language Act Section 21.
