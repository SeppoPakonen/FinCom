# PRH: Publishing Documents in Languages Other Than Finnish or Swedish

Notifications to the Trade Register must always be made in Finnish or Swedish, but a limited liability company may voluntarily publish information in other languages as well.

## 1. Principles of Publication
* The register information will include a notation of the additional languages used.
* Official translations according to language legislation are required.
* Filing notifications on paper forms is not possible starting from 1.1.2026.

## 2. Filing
* **Online form:** [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin)
* **New companies:** Form Y1 + attachment 1.
* **Change notification:** Form Y4 + attachment 13.
* **Attachments:** Receipt of payment, documents in foreign language and official translations.

## 3. Costs
* **Free:** As part of establishment or change notification.
* **55 euros:** If filed as a separate matter using the online form.

## 4. Foreign Filers (no Finnish personal identity code)
A citizen of an EU country may file electronically if:
1. They have an identification tool from another EU country.
2. The notification concerns a limited liability company or branch office.

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 19 and Language Act Section 21.
