# PRH: Limited Liability Company and Branch in the EEA

Since 2023, business registers in the European Economic Area (EEA) have been exchanging company information automatically. PRH registers and sends information automatically.

## 1. Finnish companies abroad
* PRH receives information about branches of Finnish limited liability companies (Ltd/PLC) in other EEA countries.
* The information is registered as such in the data of the Finnish parent company.

## 2. Foreign branches in Finland
* PRH sends information about Finnish branches to their foreign parent registers.
* PRH receives information about responsible persons from foreign parent companies and registers them in the data of the Finnish branch.
* **Tip:** Branches should still report changes in responsible persons themselves to ensure the accuracy of information.

## 3. Exchangeable information
* Company name, registration number and home country.
* **EUID:** European Unique Identifier.
* Responsible persons: Name, citizenship, date of birth and home address.

## 4. Monitoring and search
* Notification of registration is sent by email if the company has provided an address to PRH.
* European company information can be searched in the BRIS system: [European Justice Portal](https://e-justice.europa.eu).

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 20 c.
