# Computer User AB Card: Qualification Content and Objectives

This document defines the module-specific objectives and key content of the AB Card module, which is part of the ICT sector professional qualification.

---

## 1. Overview
The qualification provides skills for efficient use of information technology in working life. It is suitable for continuing education of the working-age population after the A Card.
- **Structure:** 1 mandatory and 3 elective modules.
- **Demonstration exam:** 2 h 15 min.

---

## 2. Computer Program Interoperability and Data Sharing (MAND.)

### Objectives
- Versatile use of data entered once in different programs.
- Management of file formats, data transfer methods (linking, embedding), and group programs (email, calendar).
- Workstation maintenance, protection, and backups.

### Key Content
- **Operating system:** Customization, network stations, installations, print queues.
- **Data transfer:** Linking, embedding, import/export, ready interfaces.
- **Office tools:** Image archives, formula editors, graphics.
- **Group programs:** Email settings, archiving, real-time communication, time management (calendars, task lists).

---

## 3. Text Processing (Elective)

### Objectives
- Creation of uniform document templates (bases) and forms for the company.
- Management of multi-page and long documents (table of contents, indexes, styles).

### Key Content
- **Formatting:** Styles, document standards, column layout, division.
- **Automation:** Merge functions (mass mailings, labels), macros, tables.

---

## 4. Spreadsheet Calculation (Elective)

### Objectives
- Creation, analysis, and visualization of calculation models.
- Use of advanced functions and macros.

### Key Content
- **Charts:** Layout, editing, and linking to other programs.
- **Functions:** Min, Max, Average, If, SumIf, CountIf, CountA, Lookup, Calendar, and Financial functions.
- **Analysis:** Goal seek, filtering, cross-tabulation, matrix calculation, conditional formatting.

---

## 5. Databases (Elective)

### Objectives
- Design, creation, and ensuring accuracy of relational databases.
- Implementation of customized forms, reports, and queries.

### Key Content
- **Structure:** Relations of multiple tables, primary and foreign keys, referential integrity.
- **Queries:** AND/OR logic, search, update, delete, and parameter queries. Utilization of SQL language.
- **Automation:** Macros, program code, dropdown menus.

---

## 6. Publishing and Image Processing (Elective)

### Objectives
- Production of print materials (fitting text and images).
- Editing and optimization of images for different publishing channels.

### Key Content
- **Theory:** Printing process, file formats (PDF), copyright, scanning.
- **Practice:** Sheet definition, text flow, drawing tools, color management, filters.

---

## 7. Web Publishing Management (Elective)

### Objectives
- Updating, building, and information retrieval from WWW pages.
- Effective use of FTP services and search bots.

### Key Content
- **Production:** HTML code editing, editor use, image optimization for the web.
- **Communication:** Email and discussion groups in problem solving.

---

## 8. Digital Communication (Elective)

### Objectives
- Understanding multimedia possibilities and compiling digital presentations.
- Management of media elements (audio, video, animation).

### Key Content
- **Elements:** GIF animations, digital video, audio file formats and transfer.
- **Process:** Layered content and interactivity.

---

## 9. Telecommunications (Elective)

### Objectives
- Management of telecommunications terminology, telecommunication networks, and wireless data transfer.
- Understanding data and voice flow in different networks.

### Key Content
- **Basics:** Information theory and basic techniques.
- **Networks:** Structure and services of telecommunication and data transfer networks.
- **Mobile:** Radio systems, basic structure of mobile networks, and data transfer.
