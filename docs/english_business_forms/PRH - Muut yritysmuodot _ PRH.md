# PRH: Financial Statements for Other Business Forms

This guide concerns filing financial statements in the Trade Register for savings banks, insurance and mortgage institutions, and European economic interest groupings.

## 1. Schedule and Responsibility
* **Deadline:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.
* **Signatory:** The responsible person registered in the Trade Register. Procuration does not entitle to signing.

## 2. Notification (YTJ Service)
* The notification is made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* **Cost:** Free of charge.
* **Contents:** The notification must contain all legally required financial statement documents.

## 3. After Registration
* PRH may register the financial statement automatically.
* The register extract is sent to the address reported by the company.
* Financial statement information is public and available in the [Virre information service](https://virre.prh.fi).

---
**Tip:** You can authorize an agent (e.g., accounting firm) to file the notification on your behalf in the YTJ service.
