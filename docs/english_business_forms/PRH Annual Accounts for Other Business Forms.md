# PRH: Annual Accounts for Other Business Forms

This guide concerns filing annual accounts to the Trade Register for savings banks, insurance and mortgage associations, and European economic interest groupings.

## 1. Schedule and Responsibility
* **Deadline:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.
* **Signatory:** The responsible person registered in the Trade Register. Procuration does not authorize signing.

## 2. Notification (YTJ service)
* Notification is made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* **Fee:** Free of charge.
* **Contents:** The notification must include all legally required annual accounts documents.

## 3. After Registration
* PRH may register the annual accounts automatically.
* The register extract is sent to the address reported by the company.
* Annual accounts information is public and available from the [Virre information service](https://virre.prh.fi).

---
**Tip:** You can authorize an agent (e.g., accounting firm) to handle the notification on your behalf in the YTJ service.
