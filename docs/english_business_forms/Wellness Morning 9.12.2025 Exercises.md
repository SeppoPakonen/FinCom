# Wellness Morning 9.12.2025: Exercises

Welcome to the students' wellness morning! The objective of this morning is to pause and reflect on your own well-being and do exercises that support coping.

---

## 1. Breathing Exercises
Breathing exercises help calm the mind and body, especially when thoughts are chaotic or emotions are strong.
- **Exercise:** Familiarize yourself with the exercises and do at least one.
- **Link:** [Mental Health House: Breathing Exercises](https://www.mielenterveystalo.fi/en/self-care/young-adults-anxiety-self-care-program/6-breathing-exercises)

---

## 2. Resources
People get strength from different things. Identifying your own resources is the first step to strengthening them.
- **Exercise:** Create your own **resource circle** (e.g., in your notebook). Consider what things bring you energy.
- **Link:** [Health Village: Identifying Your Own Resources](https://www.terveyskyla.fi/kuntoutumistalo/kuntoutumistalon-oppaat/opas-omien-voimavarojen-tunnistamiseen-ja-vahvistamiseen/mista-on-kys)

---

## 3. Recovery
Recovery can be compared to charging batteries. It is essential for maintaining healthy lifestyle habits.
- **Exercise:** Try recovery cards and find methods suitable for you to end your study or work day.
- **Link:** [Recovery Cards (PDF)](https://liikkuvatyoelama.fi/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2025/08/Palautumiskortit-tulostettavaksi_A4.pdf)

---

## 4. Gratitude
Practicing gratitude is, according to research, one of the most effective ways to reduce negativity and strengthen self-esteem.
- **Exercise:** Write down or consider in your mind: **What things are you grateful for in your life right now?**
- **Benefits:** Shifts attention away from failures and helps notice the good things in daily life.

---

## 5. Mindfulness and Body Meditation
The connection between the body and mind is strong. Body meditation strengthens presence and improves connection with oneself.
- **Exercise:** Do exercise no. 2: **Body Meditation**.
- **Link:** [Mieli.fi: Moments of Presence](https://mieli.fi/en/strengthen-mental-health/exercises/moments-of-presence/)

---
