# Template: Marketing Plan (Comprehensive)

This template is suitable for strategic planning of marketing and corporate image.

## 1. Current Situation Analysis
* **Business Idea and Competitive Advantage:** Why does the customer choose us?
* **Industry Situation:** Market size, growth and competitive landscape.
* **Stakeholders:** Role of owners, employees and partners.

## 2. Customer and Partnership Review
* **Customer Segments:** Who do we sell to and what are their characteristics?
* **Bargaining Power:** Importance of customers and suppliers and dependency relationships.
* **Product Significance:** Sales volume, margin and competitiveness by product.

## 3. SWOT Analysis and Environmental Changes
* **SWOT:** Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats.
* **Environment:** How are primary and secondary market areas changing?

## 4. Strategic Choices and Image Objectives
* **Strategies:** Cost efficiency, differentiation or narrow focus.
* **Image Objectives:**
    - **Products:** What impression do the products convey?
    - **Pricing:** Cheap, quality or value for money?
    - **Personnel:** How do employees perceive the company?
    - **Environment:** How is responsibility visible externally?

## 5. Future Outlook (5 years)
* **Growth Plan:** Which areas to abandon, where to invest in new?
* **Financial Objective:** Profitability and market position at the end of the period.
