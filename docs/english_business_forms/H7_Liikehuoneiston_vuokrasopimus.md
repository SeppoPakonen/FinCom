## Terms of the Lease Agreement (Summary)

1. **Condition of the premises:** The tenant has inspected the premises and accepts them in their current condition unless otherwise agreed.
2. **Use and maintenance:** The tenant is obligated to carefully maintain the premises and comply with the property's house rules and official regulations. Permission is required to install signs.
3. **Maintenance:** The tenant is responsible for wear and repair needs caused by their own operations.
4. **Renovation work:** No renovation or improvement work may be carried out in the premises without the landlord's written permission.
5. **Renovations:** The landlord has the right to carry out repair work. Two weeks' notice is given for routine work and two months' notice for extensive work.
6. **Rent reduction:** The tenant has the right to rent reduction only when the premises are out of use if the cause is attributable to the landlord (excluding strikes, force majeure, etc.).
