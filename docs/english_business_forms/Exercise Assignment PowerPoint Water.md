# Exercise Assignment: PowerPoint (Water)

The objective of this assignment is to create a basic presentation about water and its properties.

## Presentation Requirements
* **Slide count:** 4–5 slides.
* **Design:** Use an appropriate design template (Theme) or background color.
* **Illustrations:** Add images related to the topic to the presentation.

## Slide Content

### Slide 1: Title Page
* Topic: PowerPoint Course / Exercise 2
* Your name and date.

### Slide 2: Water in Facts
* Chemical formula: H2O.
* Consists of hydrogen and oxygen.
* Properties: Clear, colorless, and tasteless liquid.
* Melting point 0°C, boiling point 100°C.
* **Reflection:** If 55–80% of human body weight is water, how many liters of water are in you?

### Slide 3: Water Cycle
* Evaporation from seas.
* Precipitates to land.
* Forms freshwater bodies.
* Flows back to the sea.

### Slide 4: Humans and Water
* **Water loss:**
    - Through skin: 0.5 l
    - Through breathing: 0.5 l
    - Otherwise: 1.5 l
* **Water intake:**
    - From food: 0.7 l
    - From drinks: 1.5 l
    - From metabolism: 0.3 l
