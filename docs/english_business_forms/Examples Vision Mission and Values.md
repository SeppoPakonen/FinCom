# Examples: Vision, Mission and Values

This compilation contains examples of visions, missions and values of well-known companies as inspiration for building your own strategy.

## 1. Finnish Companies

### **Marimekko**
* **Vision:** The world's most captivating design brand and pioneer in pattern expertise.
* **Values:** Working together, authenticity, courage, fairness and joie de vivre.

### **Valio**
* **Mission:** We create well-being and taste experiences responsibly.
* **Vision:** Valio is the world's most innovative dairy and food company.

### **Fazer**
* **Mission:** Food that matters.
* **Vision:** Towards perfect days.
* **Values:** Nordic freedom, fearless creativity and genuine relationships.

### **Nokia**
* **Vision:** Create technology that connects the world.
* **Values:** Trust, challenge and continuous learning.

## 2. International Companies

### **Tesla**
* **Mission:** Accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

### **IKEA**
* **Vision:** Create a better everyday life for ordinary people.

### **Patagonia**
* **Mission:** Save our home planet (We're in business to save our home planet).

---

## 3. What can be learned from these?
1. **Brevity is an asset:** The best vision is one that every employee can memorize.
2. **Evoke emotion:** Mission answers the question "Why do we exist?" and gives meaning to work.
3. **Concreteness:** Values should be visible in daily choices and customer interactions.
