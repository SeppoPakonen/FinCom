# PRH: Mandatory Electronic Processing in the Trade Register from 2026

Starting January 1, 2026, most companies must handle Trade Register affairs electronically.

## 1. Who is affected by the change?
* **Mandatory:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives, general partnerships and limited partnerships.
* **Not affected:** Business name entrepreneurs (private entrepreneurs), associations and foundations.

## 2. What must be reported electronically?
* Formation, change and termination notifications.
* Financial statements and various applications.
* Corrections and supplements to previous notifications.

## 3. Service Channels
* **YTJ service:** [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi).
* **PRH's online forms:** Used in situations that cannot be handled in YTJ.
* **Note:** Notifications will not be accepted by email. Processing requires strong authentication.

## 4. Why is the change being made?
* Faster registration and utilization of information.
* Fewer errors due to digital guidance.
* Based on the new Trade Register Act.
