# Elements of a Marketing Plan

Use this checklist to ensure that your company's marketing plan is comprehensive and strategically strong.

## 1. Analysis (Where are we?)
* **Industry:** Market size and trends.
* **Competitors:** Who else does the same thing and how are they performing?
* **Own resources:** SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats).

## 2. Strategy (How do we differentiate?)
* **Segmentation:** Precisely defined customer groups.
* **Positioning:** What position do we take in the customer's mind?
* **Competitive advantage:** Why does the customer choose us (price, quality, service)?

## 3. Objectives (What do we want?)
* **Sales:** Euros, quantities.
* **Brand:** Growth in awareness, desired image.
* **Customers:** New customer acquisition and customer retention.

## 4. Actions (What do we do?)
* **Product:** Range and added value factors.
* **Price:** Pricing model and discount policy.
* **Availability:** Sales channels (online store, brick-and-mortar, representatives).
* **Communication:** Advertising, social media, newsletters, PR.

## 5. Measurement (How did we succeed?)
* **KPI (Key Performance Indicators):** Trackable metrics (e.g. conversion rate, NPS).
* **Budget:** Cost management.
* **Annual calendar:** Timing of activities.
