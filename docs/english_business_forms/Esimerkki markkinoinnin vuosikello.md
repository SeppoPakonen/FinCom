# Example: Marketing Annual Calendar (Tauno Building Maintenance)

This is an example of an annual marketing plan for a small business owner (Tauno Building Maintenance).

---

## Ongoing activities (throughout the year)
- **Social media:** Post twice a week.
- **Customer register:** Continuous maintenance and updates.

---

## Monthly activities

### January (Establishment)
- Setting up a website.
- Creating Facebook and Instagram accounts.
- Printing work clothes and car decals.

### February (Visibility)
- Mapping potential market areas.
- Creating a printed A5 flyer.
- Distributing flyers to mailboxes.

### March
- Paid social media marketing (once a week).
- Door-to-door visits.

### April
- Paid social media marketing (twice a week).
- Establishing a customer register (collecting contacts and marketing permissions).

### May
- Maintaining customer information in the register.

### June
- Take 1 week of vacation.

### July
- Take 2 weeks of vacation.

### August
- Social media marketing (once a week).
- Distributing flyers to mailboxes.

### September (Active sales)
- Paid social media marketing (twice a week).
- Sending offers to existing customers via email and text message.
- Door-to-door visits.

### October
- Paid social media marketing (once a week).
- Sending offer letters to apartment buildings.

### November
- Paid social media marketing (once a week).
- Follow-up: survey after offers to apartment buildings.

### December
- Sending Christmas cards to existing customers.
- Take 1 week of vacation.
