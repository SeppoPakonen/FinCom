# Business Plan (BP) 2026 - DummyMusicCompany

This template is intended to support the establishment and development of a company.

## 1. Basic Information
* **Company name:** DummyMusicCompany
* **Business form:** Private entrepreneur (trade name)
* **Owners and their roles:** 
  - Seppo Pakonen: Music technology specialist, production manager, marketing
  - Responsible for all business areas

## 2. Business Concept and Vision
* **Mission:** Provide diverse music services that combine creativity (personalized songs and commercial music) with technical expertise (production, distribution, education).
* **Business idea:** 
  - Personalized songs to order (for private individuals and companies)
  - Commercial jingles and brand music
  - Music production and recording services using a mobile setup
  - Distribution management through DistroKid
  - Music production education in private lessons (€30-50 / 75 min)
  - Live online sessions for artists
  - Assistance in establishing online merchandise services
* **Vision:** 
  - By 2030, known as a producer of personalized music and as an expert providing support for independent artists.
  - Build a strong brand that symbolizes creativity, technical expertise, and accessibility.
* **Values:** 
  - Quality: Each project receives personal attention and high-quality execution
  - Ethics: Honest and fair operations with customers and other parties
  - Creativity: Innovative approach to music creation and distribution
  - Accessibility: Flexible and affordable services for independent artists

## 3. Baseline Analysis
* **Customers:** 
  - Private individuals for special occasions (birthdays, weddings, etc.)
  - Small businesses that need custom commercial music
  - Independent artists without access to a studio
  - Young music students who want to learn music production
  - Content creators who need custom background music
* **Competitors:** 
  - Fiverr and other freelance marketplaces
  - Local privately-owned recording studios
  - Music production education services
  - Commercial music providers
* **Competitive advantage:** 
  - Combines creativity (personalized songs) with technical expertise (production, distribution, education)
  - Flexible, mobile setup enables operations in different locations
  - Reasonable prices without large initial costs
  - Personal service and creative approach
* **SWOT Analysis:**
  - Strengths: Technical expertise, creativity, flexibility, low cost structure
  - Weaknesses: One person handles all operations, limited marketing budget
  - Opportunities: Growing need for digital music, remote work possibilities, education market
  - Threats: Competition with large studios and platforms, economic difficulties in customer companies

## 4. Marketing and Sales
* **Channels:** 
  - Social media (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
  - Freelance marketplaces (Fiverr, Upwork)
  - Websites and portfolio
  - Direct marketing (networking, partnerships)
* **Sales target:** 
  - First year: 50 personalized songs, 10 commercial music pieces, 100 education hours
  - Financial target: €15,000 per year
* **Pricing:** 
  - Personalized songs: €50-200 depending on the project
  - Commercial music: €100-500 depending on the project
  - Production services: €25-75 per hour
  - Distribution management: €10-50 per month per artist
  - Education hours: €30-50 / 75-minute hour

## 5. Resources and Finance
* **Team:** 
  - One founder with strong technical and creative expertise
  - Possibility for part-time partnerships as needed
* **Premises and equipment:** 
  - Mobile music production station (DAW, audio interface, microphones, monitors, MIDI controller)
  - Possibility to use different locations for customer projects
  - Cloud-based tools and video conferencing
* **Budget:** 
  - Initial investments: Existing equipment
  - Operating expenses: 
    - Local transportation: €50/month
    - Websites and hosting: €10/month
    - Marketing: €0/month (initially)
  - Revenue: See sales targets in section 4

## 6. Risk Management
* Identified threats and methods to minimize them:
  - Competition: Continuously develop unique services and brand
  - Importance of individual customers: Diversify customer base
  - Technology change: Stay current with latest tools and trends
  - Equipment failure: Ensure backup equipment and/or equipment insurance
