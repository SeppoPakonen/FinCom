# PRH: Beneficial Owner Notification (Frequently Asked Questions)

This document compiles key answers to questions about notifications of actual beneficial owners to the Trade Register.

## 1. Who is a beneficial owner?
* A person who owns **more than 25%** of shares or voting rights.
* A person who exercises actual control on another basis.
* **Note:** A beneficial owner is always a person.

## 2. Who reports?
* **Mandatory:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives, and most other business forms.
* **Does not report:** Sole traders, stock exchange companies, housing companies, associations, and foundations.

## 3. Why must the notification be made?
* Based on the money laundering act and EU directives.
* Helps banks and accounting firms identify their customers.
* Negligence can result in fines or removal of the company from the Trade Register.

## 4. How is the notification made?
* **Method:** Electronically in the YTJ service at [www.ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi).
* **Fee:** Free of charge.
* **Signature:** Board member, managing director, or authorized agent.

## 5. When must the notification be made?
* Immediately after establishing the company.
* Whenever beneficial owner information changes.
* If the company has not yet made the notification.

---
**Verification:** You can check the status of the notification free of charge in the [Virre information service](https://virre.prh.fi).
