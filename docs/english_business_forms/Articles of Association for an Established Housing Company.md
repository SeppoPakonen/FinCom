# Articles of Association for an Established Housing Company

A housing company must always have articles of association. The articles of association are registered in the Trade Register when the company is established. The articles of association is a public document from which anyone can obtain information.

You report the information of the articles of association at the same time as you make the establishment notification of the housing company in the YTJ service. The service creates the articles of association. [Read the establishment notification instructions.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/establishingacompany/establishingahousingcompany.html)

## What kind of articles of association does the YTJ service create?

Structure of the articles of association created by the YTJ service:

* company's trade name (Section 1)
* registered office (Section 2)
* business activity (Section 3)
* apartment specifications (Section 4)
* signature method (Section 5)
* other articles of association information (provisions from Section 6 onwards).

[View an example of articles of association.](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/establishingacompany/establishingahousingcompany/articlesofassociation/example_articles_of_association.html)

## What information must be included in the articles of association?

The articles of association of a housing company must contain information at least on the following:

* company's trade name and registered office (a municipality located in Finland)
* company's business activity, i.e., the location of the buildings and properties managed by the company and the basis of management
* information on the location, number, area, purpose, number of rooms, and shares of the apartments
* information on spaces managed by the company
* grounds for determining the company fee as well as who determines the amount and payment method of the fee.

There must be a provision on the financial year either in the articles of association or in the articles of incorporation.

The articles of association may also contain provisions on the following:

* signature method (authorized signatories)
* redemption clause (read more in Section 5 of Chapter 2 of the Housing Company Act)
* right of the company's or shareholders' creditors to object to amendments to the articles of association (read more in Section 7 of Chapter 17 of the Housing Company Act).

## How do you report the company's business activity?

Section 3 of the articles of association must contain the business activity reported to the company's Trade Register, i.e., information on the location and basis of management of the buildings and properties managed by the company.

In the YTJ service, provide one of the following property identification details:

* property identifier (in the format 123-456-1234-5678), go to the service: [Find property identifier Opens in a new tab](https://www.maanmittauslaitos.fi/en/properties/services/find-property-identifier)
* cadastral identifier (in the format 123-456-1234-5678-M601)
* facility identifier (lease right, in the format 123-456-1234-5678-L1).

For property identification matters, contact the National Land Survey. [Go to maanmittauslaitos.fi website. Opens in a new tab](https://www.maanmittauslaitos.fi/)

## How do you report apartment specifications?

Section 4 of the articles of association must contain the apartment specifications, i.e., information on the buildings, apartments, parking spaces, and other spaces.

You provide information on parking spaces and other spaces belonging to the apartment under the apartment section. Parking spaces and other spaces managed with their own shares are reported in a separate section depending on whether they are located in the building or outside the buildings.

Proceed as follows in the YTJ service:

1. Start by adding a building and its address.
2. Add an apartment and its basic information under the address.
3. Provide the apartment's share information. When making the establishment notification, you also provide information on the shareholder of the share.
4. Provide information on parking spaces and other spaces belonging to the apartment if necessary.
5. Add information on parking spaces and other spaces in the building.
6. Add information on parking spaces and other spaces outside the buildings.

All information you provide will appear in the articles of association except for [shareholder information](https://www.prh.fi/en/companiesandorganisations/establishingacompany/establishingahousingcompany/shareholders.html).

## How do you report other information in the articles of association?

Write the other provisions of the articles of association in the service's text field.

The signature method you select appears as Section 5 of the articles of association. Save the other provisions starting from Section 6.   
  
If you do not select a signature method, start the other provisions from Section 5.   
  
You can copy the provisions to be added to the text field from your computer's word processing program. Remove document formatting before pasting the text into the field.

## What kind of attachment?

Sometimes the articles of association contain an attachment, such as a map or liability distribution table. Add the attachment with the establishment notification in the YTJ service.

The attachment must be one file, but it may contain multiple pages. The pages must be A4-sized and high quality.

Accepted file formats are pdf, doc and docx.

Note! Paper printouts from the Trade Register's information service are always black and white. For this reason, the articles of association attachment cannot use colors to indicate, for example, different areas of a map, but they must be expressed in another way.
