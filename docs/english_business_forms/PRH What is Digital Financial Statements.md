# PRH: What is digital financial statements?

Digital financial statements refers to financial statements that are formatted in a machine-readable and structured format using the IXBRL standard.

## 1. How does it work?
* **IXBRL:** Combines text and numbers into machine-readable code. The report is simultaneously a human-readable XHTML page and machine-processable data.
* **Taxonomy:** Defines a standardized structure that ensures all statutory information is included.

## 2. Benefits to the company
* **Speed:** Registration occurs at best already the next working day.
* **Quality:** Taxonomy guides completion and reduces errors.
* **Financing:** Facilitates obtaining credit ratings and foreign financing (data is language-independent).

## 3. Benefits to information users
* Enables automatic comparison and analysis of information without manual work.
* Ensures the integrity of the material and the finding of statutory sections.

## 4. When is it mandatory?
* Mandatory for sustainability reporting companies.
* Possible and recommended for all limited liability companies.
