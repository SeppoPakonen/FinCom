# Market Research: Online Shopping Survey 2023 (Svea)

The survey maps Finnish consumers' online shopping behavior, payment method preferences, and obstacles in the purchasing process.

## 1. Key Findings
* **Shopping pace:** 83% of Finns made online purchases in 2022. However, growth has leveled off ("saturation point").
* **Payment methods:** Finland is a country of online banking (82% have used it). Mobile payments (MobilePay, ApplePay, etc.) are growing rapidly (+31.5% in volume).
* **Invoice and installment payments:** Popularity is increasing, especially in larger purchases. Average invoice purchase 283 €, installment payment 631 €.
* **Average purchase:** The average across all payment methods is approximately 175 €.

## 2. Why does the purchase transaction interrupt?
As many as 73% have left a purchase unfinished. Reasons:
1. **Shipping costs:** Too expensive or unclear.
2. **Change of mind:** Shopping carts are often used just as "memory aids" or for price comparison.
3. **Payment methods:** Desired payment method is missing (15% of interruptions).
4. **Registration:** Mandatory account creation is considered cumbersome.

## 3. Returns and Sustainability
* **Return rate:** One in three has returned a product. Clothing and footwear are the most common return categories.
* **Domestic preference:** 95% prefer Finnish online stores for reliability, security, and supporting Finnish jobs.
* **Responsibility:** Environmental friendliness and ethics are increasingly influencing purchasing decisions.

## 4. Tips for Online Retailers 2023–2026
* **Optimize the purchase path:** Make purchasing easy without mandatory registration.
* **Show prices upfront:** Announce shipping costs and possible additional fees immediately.
* **Diverse payment methods:** Offer online banking, cards, invoice, and mobile payments.
* **Differentiate:** Highlight your online store's competitive advantages (e.g., fast delivery, free returns, domestic origin).
