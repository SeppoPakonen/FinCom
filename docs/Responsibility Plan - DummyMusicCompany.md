# Responsibility Plan - DummyMusicCompany

## Environmental Responsibility

### Reducing Digital Footprint
- Use cloud-based tools to minimize the need for physical devices
- Favor online meetings instead of traveling
- Use energy-efficient devices and turn them off after use

### Sustainable Music Production
- Use digital distribution channels to reduce the need for physical CDs and printed materials
- Favor durable equipment with a long lifespan
- Recycle old equipment properly

## Social Responsibility

### Community and Participation
- Offer discounts to young artists and hobby-level projects
- Participate in local cultural events by providing music services
- Offer free mini-consultations to local independent artists

### Workplace Ethics
- Fair and transparent pricing for customers
- Proper contracts for all projects
- Respect for intellectual property rights of clients and third parties
- Contract terms that protect both the customer and service provider

## Ethical Operations

### Honesty and Transparency
- Clear delivery terms and project progress
- Honest marketing without exaggerated claims
- Transparency to customers about how their information is used

### Data Protection and Privacy
- Secure storage of customer materials
- Clear terms for continued use of materials
- Removal of customer materials upon request

## Company Values from a Responsibility Perspective

### Quality and Responsibility
- Each project is treated individually and responsibly
- Strive to create music that has a positive impact on listeners
- Respect the integrity of the customer's creative work

### Sustainable Growth
- Continuously seek ways to reduce environmental impact
- Invest in expertise and technology that enables more efficient operations
- Build long-term partnerships with customers

## Objectives and Metrics

### Responsibility Goals for 2026
- 100% digital operations without printed materials
- 50% discount for young artists
- 5 local collaborative projects
- 0 improper disposal of equipment

### Monitoring and Development
- Annual assessment of responsibility
- Customer feedback collection after each project
- Continuous improvement and adoption of new sustainable practices
