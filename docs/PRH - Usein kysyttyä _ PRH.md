**1. Q: What notifications and applications does mandatory electronic filing cover?**

All notifications and applications arriving at PRH from 1.1.2026 onwards. These include, among others, the following:

* registration notifications
* change notifications
* dissolution notifications
* applications
* financial statements
* corrections and supplements to trade register notifications.

**2. Q: I am a sole trader. Do I have to file the notification electronically starting from 2026?**

No. Sole traders (private entrepreneurs) as well as associations and foundations registered in the trade register may file notifications on paper forms.

**3. Q: Why can't I file via email?**

Email does not require strong electronic identification, unlike the YTJ service and online forms.

**4. Q: What happens if I send the notification by letter in 2026?**

We will not process paper notifications and applications received from 1.1.2026 onwards if the business form is anything other than a sole trader, association or foundation.

**5. Q: I filed a paper notification and received
a request to file via the YTJ service. I had already paid
the processing fee and now I had to pay the notification
again in the YTJ service. Can I get the excess fee
back?**

Yes, you can. You received a request to make the notification in the YTJ service. Your request contains detailed instructions.

**6. Q: I had already paid the processing fee
but filed the notification in the YTJ service and had to pay
the notification again. Can I get the excess fee back?**

Yes, you can. Request a refund by sending the payment receipt and refund information to ilmoitusmaksut(at)prh.fi

**7. Q: I need to correct a trade register notification. Do I have to make the correction electronically?**

Yes, you must, if you sent the notification in 2026 and you are not a sole trader. You can send the correction or supplement in the YTJ service. Follow the instructions in the correction request you received.

**8. Q: Do I have to correct or
supplement the notification electronically if I sent the notification
on paper form in November 2025?**

No need to. Follow the instructions in the correction request you received.

**9. Q: I need to correct the financial statements electronically. What do I do?**

Report the financial statements completely anew in the YTJ service. Read the instructions [on supplementing financial statements.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/tilinpaatokset/miten_taydennat_ja_korjaat_suoraan_kaupparekisteriin_lahettamaasi_tilinpaatosta.html)

**11. Q: None of the responsible persons has Finnish or other EU identification tools and no possibility to obtain them. What do we do?**

In this situation, an authorized contact person files the notification.
Authorization is given by one of the company's responsible persons, for example
a board member or CEO. Read more [from the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/kaupparekisteri-pakollinen-sahkoinen-asiointi/erityistilanteet.html)

**12. Q: Our company's authorized representatives are companies. What do we do?**

A person authorized to represent the company, for example the CEO
files the notification using the online form. Reporting for these companies
in the YTJ service will become possible during 2026. Read more
[from the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/kaupparekisteri-pakollinen-sahkoinen-asiointi/erityistilanteet.html)

**13. Q: I cannot get online banking credentials to file the notification electronically. What do I do?**

Authorize a contact person to file on your behalf. Read more [from the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/kaupparekisteri-pakollinen-sahkoinen-asiointi/erityistilanteet.html)

**14. Q: Can a legal counsel file the notification on behalf of the responsible persons?**

They can prepare the notification ready for signing in the YTJ service. After that, the responsible person goes to sign the notification. Read more [about signing in the YTJ service from the ytj.fi page. Opens in a new tab](https://www.ytj.fi/index/ilmoittaminen-ytj-palvelussa/mitatarvitsetsahkoiseenilmoittamiseen/ilmoituksen_allekirjoittaminen.html)

**15. Q: I don't speak Finnish or Swedish and therefore cannot use the YTJ service or online forms. What do I do?**

The official languages of the Trade Register are Finnish and Swedish. You can request help for example from [Business Finland advisory service. Opens in a new tab](https://www.suomi.fi/ohjeet-ja-tuki/tuki-ja-neuvonta/yritys-suomi-neuvonta) or [local new enterprise center. Opens in a new tab](https://uusyrityskeskus.fi/)

You can also authorize a contact person to file on your behalf. Read more [from the special situations guide.](https://www.prh.fi/fi/yrityksetjayhteisot/kaupparekisteri-pakollinen-sahkoinen-asiointi/erityistilanteet.html)
