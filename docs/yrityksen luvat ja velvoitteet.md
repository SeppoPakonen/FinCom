# Business Permits, Registers and Obligations

Starting a business requires several notifications and possibly industry-specific permits.

## 1. Basic Registers
* **Trade Register:** Mandatory for most businesses.
* **Tax Administration registers:** Advance tax register, VAT register and employer register.
* **Business ID:** Obtained immediately after submitting the establishment notification.

## 2. Industry-Specific Permits
Check if you need permits from the following authorities:
* **Valvira/AVI:** Social and healthcare sector, alcohol.
* **Finnish Food Authority:** Food industry, primary production.
* **Tukes:** Chemicals, electrical work, elevators.
* **Police Department:** Security sector, fundraising.

## 3. Professional Qualifications and Passes
Certain industries require personal certificates:
* **Hygiene certificate:** Food handling facilities.
* **Serving license:** Alcohol service.
* **Occupational safety card:** Construction and industrial sectors.
* **Regulated professions:** E.g. doctors, teachers, lawyers (require recognition of qualifications).

## 4. Environmental and Waste Management Obligations
* **Environmental permit:** For operations that may cause pollution.
* **Waste management:** Obligation to arrange waste sorting and recycling (also producer responsibility for certain products).
* **Chemical safety:** Instructions for safe use and risk management.
