# Professional Touch Service: Student User Guide

Welcome to studying responsible and sustainable practices in your field. This guide will assist you in using the service.

---

## 1. Login Credentials and Access

Your teacher has two options to add you to the course:

### 1.1. Registration to group via link
Your teacher shares a link (e.g. via email or Moodle).
1. Click the link.
2. Enter the required information.
3. Click **Create credentials and join group**.
4. You will be directed to the *My Courses* page and can begin studying.

### 1.2. Logging in with teacher-created credentials
If the teacher has created credentials for you, you will have received an email containing login information.
1. Log in at: [https://kadenjalki.fi/login/](https://kadenjalki.fi/login/).
2. Enter your email address and the password you received in the email.
3. **Note!** Be careful not to copy extra spaces into the password.
4. If you forget your password, use the **Forgot password?** function.
5. Change your password on the first login on the *Profile* page.

---

## 2. Profile Information and Management

You can find your personal information in the upper right corner under **Profile**.

### 2.1. Editing User Information
In the profile, you can edit:
- First name, last name and username (note: username cannot be changed).
- Email address (should be updated after graduation so you can still download certificates later).
- Password.
- Profile picture.

### 2.2. Competency Certificates
You can find all your completed certificates in your profile. You can download them as PDF files to your computer.

### 2.3. Personal Data Management (GDPR)
You have the right to manage your data:
- You can request a summary of collected data.
- You can request deletion of your data if your educational institution or employer no longer requires retention of educational records.

---

## 3. Completing the Course

You can find your courses under **My Courses**.
- **Green indicator:** Course completed.
- **Blue indicator:** Course in progress.

### 3.1. Course Structure
Courses are divided into **modules**, which consist of **lessons**.
- Studying one lesson takes approximately 5–15 minutes.
- After going through the content, click **Mark as completed**.
- If sustainability is new to you, start with the *Introduction to Sustainable Development* course.

### 3.2. Assignments and Tests
1. **Reflection assignments:** Encourage thinking about sustainability in work. Do not affect passing, but the teacher may require completion.
2. **Competency tests:** Each module ends with a test (6–12 questions).
    - Passing requirement: **80 % of points**.
    - If you don't pass, you can retake immediately or return to review the material.

### 3.3. Progress Tracking
The progress bar at the top of the page shows your status. The *Breadcrumb* indicates where you are in the course.

### 3.4. Receiving Certificate
The course is completed when all competency tests have been passed.
1. Complete the final test.
2. Answer the feedback survey.
3. Download the competency certificate or check your email.

---

## 4. Providing Feedback

You can provide feedback through the **Give Feedback** button on each page. Feedback is especially valuable if:
- Content was interesting or irrelevant.
- Text contains difficult words or is too challenging.
- You notice technical issues.
