# PRH: Mandatory Electronic Service 2026

Starting from January 1, 2026, most companies must handle Trade Register services electronically.

## 1. Who does this change affect?
* **Mandatory:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives, open companies and limited partnerships.
* **Not affected:** Sole traders (private entrepreneurs), associations and foundations.

## 2. What must be reported electronically?
* Establishment, change and termination notifications.
* Financial statements and various applications.
* Corrections and supplements to previous notifications.

## 3. Service Channels
* **YTJ service:** [ytj.fi](https://www.ytj.fi).
* **PRH's online forms:** Used in situations that cannot be handled in YTJ.
* **Note:** Notifications will not be accepted via email. Service requires strong authentication.

## 4. Why is this change made?
* Faster registration and utilization of information.
* Fewer errors due to digital guidance.
* Based on the new Trade Register Act.
