# Template: Business Plan (Starting Company)

This template is intended for the company founder to plan the business idea and operations.

## 1. Entrepreneur's Background
* **Expertise:** Education, work experience and professional skills.
* **Motivation:** Why are you becoming an entrepreneur and what are your strengths?

## 2. Business Idea (What, for whom, how?)
* **Summary:** Brief description of the operations.
* **Products and services:** What exactly is sold?

## 3. Sales and Marketing
* **Market:** Customer numbers and demand situation.
* **Competitors:** 3 most important competitors.
* **Tools:** Logo, business cards, website, social media, brochures, vehicle graphics, etc.
* **Customer acquisition:** How do you get your first customer?

## 4. Implementation Plan
* **Investments:** Machines, equipment and premises.
* **Production:** Where are raw materials obtained from? Will subcontracting be used?
* **Responsibility:** How do you consider the environment (energy, recycling)?

## 5. Personnel and Finances
* **Team:** Required workforce and recruitment method.
* **Financial management:** How is invoicing and accounting handled?
* **Financing:** Own capital, loans and collateral.

## 6. Risks and Goals
* **Risk management:** Most important threats and their minimization.
* **Main goals:** Where is the company in 5 years?
