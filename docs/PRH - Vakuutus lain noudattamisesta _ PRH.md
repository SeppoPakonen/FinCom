# Certificate of Compliance with the Law (for establishing residential real estate companies and mutual real estate companies)

When submitting the registration notification for a residential real estate company or a mutual real estate company, you provide a certificate from the board members and the property manager or managing director regarding compliance with the law.

This page contains an example of the certificate that you attach to the registration notification for a residential real estate company or a mutual real estate company.

## What information must be included in the certificate?

The certificate must contain the following items:

* who certifies (in addition to the board members, either the property manager or the managing director)
* which law has been followed in the establishment (the Residential Real Estate Companies Act or the Limited Liability Companies Act).

## Example certificate

[Here you can write the company's trade name and Business ID]

We, the undersigned members of the company's board and the property manager/managing director [choose one] certify that in the establishment, the provisions of the Residential Real Estate Companies Act/Limited Liability Companies Act [choose one] have been followed.

Date [Write the date here]

Signatures and name details of all board members as well as the property manager or managing director

## How do you provide the certificate?

You provide the certificate in the YTJ service as an attachment either when making the notification or later, after obtaining the Business ID.
