# PRH: Ltd. and Branch Office in the European Economic Area (EEA)

Trade registers in the European Economic Area (EEA) exchange company information with each other starting from 2023. PRH registers and sends information automatically.

## 1. Finnish companies abroad
* PRH receives information about subsidiaries of Finnish limited liability companies (Oy/Oyj) in other EEA countries.
* The information is registered as such in the information of the Finnish parent company.

## 2. Foreign branches in Finland
* PRH sends information about Finnish branches to their foreign parent registers.
* PRH receives information about responsible persons of foreign parent companies and registers them in the information of the Finnish branch.
* **Tip:** The branch should still report changes in responsible persons itself to ensure the information is up to date.

## 3. Information exchanged
* Company name, register number, and home country.
* **EUID number:** European unique identifier.
* Responsible persons: Name, citizenship, date of birth, and home address.

## 4. Monitoring and Retrieval
* Notification of registration is sent by email if the company has reported an address to PRH.
* European company information can be searched from the BRIS system: [European justice portal](https://e-justice.europa.eu).

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 20c.
