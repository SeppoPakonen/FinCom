# V2: Self-Supervision Plan (Template)

A self-supervision plan is a company's own tool to ensure food safety and compliance with regulations.

---

**1. GENERAL INFORMATION**
* **Company:** __________________________
* **Responsible person:** __________________________
* **Nature of business:** [E.g. Café / Restaurant / Grocery store]

**2. RECEIVING GOODS**
* Check condition, dates and temperatures of incoming food products.
* Frozen products: at least -18 °C.
* Chilled products: max. +6 °C (or according to package labeling).

**3. STORAGE AND TEMPERATURE MONITORING**
* Temperature of refrigeration units checked and recorded daily.
* Immediate action taken on any deviations.

**4. PREPARATION AND HANDLING**
* Hot foods: internal temperature at least +70 °C (poultry +75 °C).
* Cold foods: kept cold until serving.

**5. CLEANING AND HYGIENE**
* Work areas and equipment cleaned according to cleaning schedule.
* Staff must have a valid **hygiene certificate**.
* Work clothes and hand hygiene are of primary importance.

**6. WASTE MANAGEMENT**
* Waste removed daily to appropriate containers.

**7. DEVIATIONS AND COMPLAINTS**
* All deviations and customer complaints are recorded and their causes investigated.

**8. UPDATES**
* Plan reviewed at least annually.
