# Digital Marketing

This document compiles essential information about digital marketing methods, channels, strategic models, and practical implementation.

---

## 1. Digital Operating Environment

Digital marketing is the primary way to reach customers in many industries. In Finland, smartphones and social media are a central part of daily life:
- 65% of Finns use Facebook 3–7 times a week.
- 75% have purchased furniture or household goods online; 88% have purchased clothes online.
- Websites serve as the company's digital home base, to which visibility and usability must be invested.

---

## 2. Digital Marketing Methods

### Search Engine Marketing
1. **Search Engine Optimization (SEO):** Long-term activity to improve organic (unpaid) visibility. Requires quality content, proper keywords, and technical expertise.
2. **Search Keyword Marketing (SEM):** Paid, sales-focused advertising (e.g., Google Ads). The ad rises to the top of search results with selected keywords.

### Display or Banner Advertising
Embedded ads (images/text) on news sites, blogs, and YouTube. Reaches those who don't use social media as well.

### Email Marketing
An effective way to market to your own customer register.
- Tools: Creamailer, Mailchimp.
- **Note:** Must comply with GDPR regulation; emails must always include an option to unsubscribe.

### Influencer Marketing
Collaboration with social media influencers.
- **Formats:** Percentage of sales, partnership agreements (affiliate), or broader campaigns.
- **Legal obligation:** Advertising purpose and collaboration must always be mentioned in publications.

## 3. Social Media Channels (2022 situation)

- **Facebook:** Particularly popular among those over 25 years old. Important for brand building and interaction. Tool: Facebook Business Manager.
- **Instagram:** The most significant channel in many fields (2.3 million users in Finland). 
    - *Feed:* square images (1080x1080).
    - *Stories:* 15 s stories (visible for 24 h).
    - *Reels:* short edited videos.
- **YouTube:** For showcasing expertise, expert videos, and tutorials.
- **TikTok:** Rapidly growing (about 1.4 million users). Keywords are authenticity and short, max 3 min videos.
- **LinkedIn:** Professional networking platform. Digital CV, recruitment, and building an expert profile.

---

## 4. Content Marketing

Content marketing is about creating content that interests the target audience.
- **Formats:** Social media posts, videos, podcasts, webinars, newsletters, guides, and articles.
- **Goal (Entertain – Persuade – Educate – Convert):** Choose format according to channel (video is most effective on social media).

### Media Types:
- **Owned media:** Websites, newsletters, own social media accounts (controllable).
- **Paid media:** Paid advertising and influencer collaboration (reach).
- **Earned media:** Recommendations, shares, press coverage (trust).

---

## 5. Strategic Models for Purchase Path Management

### AIDA Model
- **Awareness:** Increasing recognition.
- **Interest:** Providing information about benefits.
- **Desire:** Building trust.
- **Action:** Facilitating the purchase process.

### REAN Model
1. **REACH:** Search engines, content marketing, advertising.
2. **ENGAGE:** Appealing content, monitoring.
3. **ACTIVATE:** Clear purchase process, conversion optimization.
4. **NURTURE:** Post-marketing, newsletters, additional services.

---

## 6. Checklist and Steps to Take Control

### Planning checklist:
- Know your target group and choose channels accordingly.
- Ensure the message aligns with the company's strategy and value proposition.
- If creating a profile, be active. Outsource updates when necessary, but control content.
- Prepare for interaction and handle negative comments constructively.

### Steps to Take Control of Social Media (Kosonen & Janhunen):
1. **Follow:** What is discussed about your industry (e.g., Google Alerts).
2. **Goal:** Set a business goal for social media.
3. **Content:** Plan a content calendar (1/7/30 principle: check daily, content weekly, major story monthly).
4. **Strategy:** Choose channels (don't try to manage all at once).
5. **Discoverability:** Connect channels to each other and to websites.
6. **Metrics:** Define goals (e.g., conversion percentage, leads).
7. **Charm:** Participate in discussions and share success stories.
