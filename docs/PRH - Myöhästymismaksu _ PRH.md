# PRH: Late Financial Statement Fee

PRH orders a fee for financial statements reported late to the Trade Register. This guidance applies especially to fiscal years ending on or after December 1, 2024.

## 1. When is the fee ordered?
* If the company does not report its financial statement within **8 months** of the end of the fiscal year.
* Applies to limited liability companies, cooperatives and European companies.

## 2. Fee Amount (2024–2026)
The fee depends on the length of the delay:
* **Up to 2 months late:** 150 €.
* **2–4 months late:** 300 €.
* **Over 4 months late (or not at all):** 600 €.

## 3. Increases
* **Double fee:** If the financial statement is late for two or more consecutive fiscal years.
* **Large companies:** Public limited liability companies (plc) and European companies pay a double fee.

## 4. Collection
* The **Court Register Centre (ORK)** handles fee collection.
* If the fee is not paid voluntarily, it can be collected through enforcement proceedings.

---
**Avoid the fee:** Report your financial statement on time in the [YTJ service](https://www.ytj.fi).
