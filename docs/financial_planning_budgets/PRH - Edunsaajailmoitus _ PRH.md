# PRH: Ultimate Beneficial Owner Notification

Most companies must report information about their ultimate beneficial owners (persons who hold ownership or control) to the Trade Register and keep this information up to date.

## 1. What is a beneficial owner?
An ultimate beneficial owner is a **natural person** who:
* Directly or indirectly owns more than 25 % of the company's shares or voting rights.
* Otherwise exercises control over the company.
* **Note:** A beneficial owner cannot be a company, municipality, association or estate.

## 2. Who reports?
* **Mandatory:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives and some general/partnership companies.
* **Not required:** Business name entrepreneurs (Private entrepreneurs).

## 3. Timeline and Fee
* **When:** Immediately when beneficial owner information changes or if the notification has not been previously submitted.
* **Fee:** Free of charge.

## 4. Consequences of Non-Reporting
Failure to report may lead to:
* Default fee.
* Placement in investigation status.
* Removal from the Trade Register.
* Difficulties in banking (compliance with anti-money laundering law requirements).
