Market research is an essential step to ensure business success. It transforms assumptions into knowledge.

## 1. Key Research Methods
* **Surveys and Interviews:** Collect information directly from potential customers. Use tools like Microsoft Forms or conduct face-to-face interviews.
* **Observation:** Monitor customer behavior in real environments.
* **Online Research:** Utilize industry publications, statistics (e.g. Statistics Finland) and existing research reports.

## 2. Competitor Analysis
Research your competitors:
* What do they offer?
* What is their price level?
* What marketing strategies do they use?
* What do customers say about them (reviews, social media)?

## 3. Target Group Identification and Understanding
* Define who your ideal customer is.
* Research their needs, preferences and factors affecting their purchasing decisions.
* The better you understand your target group, the more effectively you can target your marketing.

## 4. Continuous Testing and Monitoring
* **A/B Testing:** Try two different versions of an ad or message to see which performs better.
* **Social Media Listening:** Monitor conversations and feedback related to your industry.
* **Test Marketing:** Pilot a product or service on a small scale before a wide release. Gather feedback and make corrections in a timely manner.

## 5. Trend Monitoring
Stay up to date:
* Technological innovations.
* Legislative changes.
* Broader changes in consumer behavior (e.g. importance of responsibility).
