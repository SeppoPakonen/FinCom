# Template: Company Year Clock

A year clock is a tool that helps you schedule recurring business tasks, campaigns and important dates.

| Month | Marketing & Sales | Finance & Administration | Other (Development, HR) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **January** | Discount sales, planning for next year | Annual notifications, tax cards | Development discussions |
| **February** | Valentine's Day theme | Advance tax review | Training |
| **March** | Spring season begins | Annual report completion | Product development |
| **April** | Easter campaigns | Tax return submission (Partnership) | Vacation planning |
| **May** | Graduation gifts, summer season | Summer worker orientation | Summer season preparation |
| **June** | Midsummer, summer events | Vacation pay calculation | Summer vacations |
| **July** | End of summer rush | | Vacation period |
| **August** | Back to school theme | Mid-year budget review | Fall planning |
| **September** | Fall campaigns, trade fairs | Advance taxes | Strategy update |
| **October** | Fall vacation, dark season themes | | Equipment maintenance |
| **November** | Black Friday, Father's Day | Next year's budgeting | Christmas parties |
| **December** | Christmas sales, gift cards | Inventory, thank you to partners | Year-end summary |

---
**Tip:** Print the year clock on a poster or share it digitally with your team. It helps prevent busy periods and ensures that important tasks are completed on time.
