# Entrepreneur's Fund and Unemployment Security (2026)

Joining the Entrepreneur's Fund is beneficial risk management that provides financial security when business operations end.

## 1. Membership Benefits
* **Earnings-related daily allowance:** Only as a fund member can you receive earnings-related security (at least 220 €/month more than Kela's basic daily allowance).
* **Pension accumulation:** From the daily allowance, you generally accumulate pension (1.5 %).
* **Services:** Webinars, training and personal counseling.

## 2. Membership Requirements (2026)
* **Status:** You are an entrepreneur covered by the unemployment security act (YEL, MYEL or co-owner with TyEL insurance).
* **Earned income:** Your annual earned income from pension insurance must be at least **15,481 €**.
* **Cost:** Membership fee is 1.7 % of the portion exceeding the insurance level of 9,600 € (minimum fee approximately 8.33 €/month).

## 3. Receiving Daily Allowance
* **Membership and entrepreneurship requirement:** At least 15 months within the previous 48 months.
* **Cessation:** Business operations must have demonstrably ended or work ceased.
* **Graduation:** The earnings-related daily allowance decreases in two stages after 40 and 170 paid days (if unemployment began after September 2, 2024).

---
**Note:** The membership fee is fully deductible in the final taxation.
