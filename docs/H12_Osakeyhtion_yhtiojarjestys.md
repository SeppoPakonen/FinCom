This is a template for the articles of association of a limited liability company, which includes a **redemption clause**. The redemption clause can restrict the transfer of shares to outsiders.

### 3. Board of directors
- The company's board of directors consists of 1–5 regular members. If there are fewer than three members, at least one alternate member must be elected.
- The board's term of office ends at the conclusion of the next ordinary general meeting following the election.

### 4. Auditors
- The company selects one auditor and one alternate auditor. If the auditor is a KHT or HTM entity, an alternate auditor is not required.

### 5. Invitation to general meeting
- The invitation to the ordinary and extraordinary general meeting must be delivered in writing to the shareholders at the address recorded in the shareholder register at least one week before the meeting.

### 6. Ordinary general meeting
The general meeting is held annually on a date determined by the board within six months of the end of the financial year. The meeting:
- Presents the financial statements and audit report.
- Decides on the approval of the income statement and balance sheet.
- Decides on granting discharge from liability to the members of the board of directors and the managing director.
- Decides on the allocation of profits.
- Elects the members of the board of directors and the auditor.

### 7. Redemption clause
If a share is transferred to someone other than the company's current shareholders, the transferee must immediately notify the board of directors. Current shareholders have the right to redeem the transferring share under the following conditions:
- **Restrictions:** The right of redemption does not apply to acquisitions based on inheritance, will or matrimonial property rights.
- **Distribution:** If several shareholders wish to redeem, the shares are distributed according to the shareholders' shareholdings.
- **Redemption price:** The price agreed between the parties, however no more than the value of the share based on the latest financial statements. In gratuitous acquisition, the price is the value based on the financial statements.
- **Time:** The redemption demand must be presented to the company within one month of the notification of the share transfer to the board of directors.
