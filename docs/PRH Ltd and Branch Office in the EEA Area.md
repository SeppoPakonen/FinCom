# PRH: Ltd. and Branch Office in the EEA Area

Trade registers in the European Economic Area (EEA) exchange company information with each other starting from 2023. PRH registers and sends information automatically.

## 1. Finnish companies abroad
* PRH receives information about subsidiaries of Finnish limited liability companies (Ltd/PLC) in other EEA countries.
* The information is registered as such in the information of the Finnish parent company.

## 2. Foreign branch offices in Finland
* PRH sends information about Finnish branch offices to their foreign parent registers.
* PRH receives information about responsible persons of foreign parent companies and registers them in the information of the Finnish branch office.
* **Tip:** The branch office should still report changes in responsible persons itself to ensure the information is up to date.

## 3. Information exchanged
* Company name, register number, and home country.
* **EUID number:** European unique identifier.
* Responsible persons: Name, nationality, date of birth, and home address.

## 4. Monitoring and retrieval
* Notification of registration is sent by email if the company has reported an address to PRH.
* European company information can be retrieved from the BRIS system: [European justice portal](https://e-justice.europa.eu).

---
**Legislation:** Trade Register Act Section 20c.
