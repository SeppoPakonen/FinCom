# PRH: Beneficial Owner Notification

Most companies must report to the Trade Register information about their actual beneficial owners (persons with ownership or control) and keep this information up to date.

## 1. What is a beneficial owner?
An actual beneficial owner is a **person** who:
* Directly or indirectly owns more than 25% of the company's shares or voting rights.
* Otherwise exercises control over the company.
* **Note:** A beneficial owner cannot be a company, municipality, association, or estate.

## 2. Who reports?
* **Mandatory:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives, and some open/limited partnerships.
* **Does not report:** Sole traders (Private entrepreneurs).

## 3. Schedule and Fee
* **When:** Immediately when beneficial owner information changes or if the notification has not been made previously.
* **Fee:** Free of charge.

## 4. Consequences of Not Reporting
Negligence can lead to:
* Penalty fee.
* Placement in investigation status.
* Removal from the Trade Register.
* Difficulties in banking (money laundering law requirements).
