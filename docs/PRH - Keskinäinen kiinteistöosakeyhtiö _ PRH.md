# PRH: Mutual Real Estate Company

A mutual real estate company (koy) is a company form where shares provide the right to manage a specific space or apartment in the company's building.

## 1. Applicable Law
* The **Housing Company Act** is generally applied.
* The articles of association may specify that the Limited Liability Companies Act is applied (either completely or partially).

## 2. Establishment
* **Deadline:** The notification must be made within **3 months** of signing the articles of incorporation.
* **Costs:** The establishment notification costs 400 â‚¬.
* **Apartment Information System:** New companies automatically belong to the system maintained by the National Land Survey.

## 3. Required Information and Attachments
* Original articles of incorporation.
* Board members' and managing director/property manager's declaration of compliance with the law.
* Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
* **Articles of Association:** Must be in electronic (structured) format. Contains among other things the apartment description (Section 4).

## 4. Notification
The notification is made electronically in the [YTJ service](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap). The Business ID is received immediately after submission.
