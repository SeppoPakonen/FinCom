# PRH: Reporting Financial Statements

Reporting financial statements to the Trade Register is a statutory obligation for most business forms, even if there has been no actual business operations.

## 1. Who is subject to the reporting obligation?
* **Always:** Limited liability companies, cooperatives, and foreign branches.
* **Under certain conditions:** Open companies, limited partnerships, and private entrepreneurs (if size thresholds are exceeded).
* **Not applicable:** Housing companies and real estate companies.

## 2. Schedule
* **Ltd and Cooperative:** At the latest 8 months after the end of the financial year. (Must first be approved at a general meeting).
* **Branches and others:** 6 months after the end of the financial year.

## 3. Costs
* **Free of charge:** When the notification is made on time in the YTJ service.
* **Paid:** If the notification is late or made with a paper form (100 €).

## 4. Consequences of Delay
* Late financial statement fee (150–600 €).
* At worst, removal of the company from the Trade Register.
