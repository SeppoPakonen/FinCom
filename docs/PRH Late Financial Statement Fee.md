# PRH: Late Financial Statement Fee

PRH imposes a fee for financial statements reported late to the Trade Register. This guide concerns financial years ending on or after 1.12.2024.

## 1. When is the fee imposed?
* If the company does not report the financial statement within **8 months** of the end of the financial year.
* Applies to limited liability companies, cooperatives, and European companies.

## 2. Fee Amount (2024–2026)
The fee depends on the duration of the delay:
* **Up to 2 months late:** 150 €.
* **2–4 months late:** 300 €.
* **Over 4 months late (or not at all):** 600 €.

## 3. Increases
* **Double fee:** If the financial statement is late for two or more consecutive financial years.
* **Large companies:** Public limited liability companies (Oyj) and European companies pay a double fee.

## 4. Collection
* The **ORK** (Register of Legal Entities Center) is responsible for collecting the fee.
* If the fee is not paid voluntarily, it can be collected by enforcement.

---
**Avoid the fee:** Report the financial statement on time in the [YTJ service](https://www.ytj.fi).
