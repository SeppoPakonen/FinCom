## 5. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this agreement will primarily be resolved through negotiations between the parties. If no resolution is reached within 1 month of identifying the dispute, the matter will be settled by an independent legal mediator acceptable to both parties. If necessary, the mediator will be chosen by lot between two candidates. The mediator must issue their decision within one month of appointment, and the decision binds both parties without possibility of appeal.
