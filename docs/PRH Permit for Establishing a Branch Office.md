# PRH: Permit for Establishing a Branch Office

If a foreign company's registered office is located outside the EEA area, it needs a permit from PRH to establish a branch office in Finland.

## 1. Granting the Permit
The permit is usually granted if the main establishment is properly registered in its home country.

## 2. Branch Office Representative
* The branch office must have a **representative** who has the right to receive notifications on behalf of the company.
* If the main establishment is from the EEA area, the representative's place of residence must be in the EEA area.
* If the main establishment is from outside the EEA area, the representative's registered office must be **in Finland**.

## 3. Application
* **Method:** With the online form at [turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin](https://turvaviesti.prh.fi/ilmoitus-kaupparekisteriin).
* **Fee:** 150 € (+ 6.50 € invoice fee).
* **Attachments:**
    - Free-form application.
    - Register extract of the main establishment.
    - Copy of articles of association.
    - Official translations to Finnish or Swedish.

## 4. Next Steps
Once the permit is received, a proper **branch establishment notification** must be made to the Trade Register and the permit decision must be attached.
