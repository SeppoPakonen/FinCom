# Template: Cooperative Rules

The cooperative's rules must be attached to the establishment notification. Below are two differently detailed templates.

## Template 1: Basic Minimal Template
This template contains only the mandatory sections required by law. Otherwise, the Cooperative Act applies.

1 § **Trade name:** [Name]  
2 § **Registered office:** [Municipality]  
3 § **Business activity:** [Description of operations]  
4 § **Representation:** The cooperative is represented by the board. The board may grant procuration or representation rights to a named person.  
5 § **Audit:** The cooperative does not have an obligation to elect an auditor unless the meeting decides otherwise.

---

## Template 2: Extended Template
This template contains general administrative provisions.

1 § **Trade name, registered office and business activity:** [Fill in details]  
2 § **Board of directors:** 1–5 regular members. If there are fewer than 3 members, at least one alternate member must be elected. The term of office is indefinite.  
3 § **Representation:** Board, chairman and managing director each individually, or two board members together.  
4 § **Meeting invitation:** Must be delivered in writing to members at least 2 months and at most 1 week before the meeting.  
5 § **Regular meeting:** Held annually within 6 months of the end of the financial year. Matters to be handled:
    1. Confirmation of the financial statements.
    2. Decision on the use of surplus.
    3. Granting discharge from liability to the board and managing director.
    4. Personnel appointments.
6 § **Audit:** Not mandatory for small cooperatives (see law).
