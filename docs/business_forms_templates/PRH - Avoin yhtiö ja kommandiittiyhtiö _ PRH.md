# PRH: General Partnership (Ay) and Limited Partnership (Ky)

A notification must always be made to the Trade Register about the establishment of a general partnership and limited partnership. The company is formed by registration.

## 1. Establishment Process
* **Deadline:** The notification must be submitted within **3 months** of signing the partnership agreement.
* **Signing:**
    - **Ay:** One of the partners.
    - **Ky:** One of the general partners.
    - A power of attorney does not entitle to sign the notification.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 300 €.
* **Additional business name:** 75 € / name.
* The fee must be paid before submitting the notification.

## 3. Required Documents
* **Form Y2** and separate **personal information form** (contains SSNs and addresses).
* Original partnership agreement.
* Statement about the appointment of managing director or procuration holder (if appointed).
* Receipt of processing fee.

## 4. Minor Partner
A person under 18 years of age may be a partner if:
1. Guardians represent them.
2. Guardians have permission from the Digital and Population Data Services Agency (**DVV**) for the action.

## 5. Reporting the Financial Year
The financial year must be reported to the Trade Register if the general partner is a limited liability company or another Ay/Ky whose general partner is a limited liability company.
