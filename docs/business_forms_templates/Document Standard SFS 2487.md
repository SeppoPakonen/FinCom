# Document Standard SFS 2487

This guide is based on the SFS 2487 standard and defines the layout, columns, and structure of multi-page documents.

---

## 1. Page Settings and Margins

- **Top margin:** 1 cm
- **Bottom margin:** At least 1 cm (recommended 2 cm)
- **Left margin:** 2 cm
- **Right margin:** At least 0.7 cm (recommended 1 cm)
- **Standard column width:** Columns are placed at 23 mm intervals, measured from the left edge of the form.
    - **Column positions (cm):** C0: 0, C1: 2.3, C2: 4.6, C3: 6.9, C4: 9.2, C5: 11.5, C6: 13.8, C7: 16.1.

---

## 2. Document Parts and Placement

Line numbers according to the standard must be adjusted to the number of lines in the header. Word starts line numbering from 1 after the header.

### Header (Example with 6-line header)
1. **Publisher/Sender:** Written in bold (except for private individuals). Location: C0.
2. **Document name:** Beginning with a capital letter (e.g., *Offer*, *Order*). Location: C3.
3. **Page number:** Only marked in multi-page documents in the format `Page no (Total pages)`, e.g., `1 (5)`. Location: C6.
4. **Document supplement:** Clarifying information, e.g., *Certificate of Employment – Temporary*. Location: C3.

### Recipient and Headings
- **Recipient:** According to the standard, begins on line 7. If the header is 6 lines, it is written on line 1 after the header.
- **Reference:** Standard line 14. For example, *Your advertisement in HS on 1.11.2009*.
- **Main heading:** Standard line 16. Written in bold.
- **Text:** According to the standard, begins on line 20. Text is indented to column position C2 (4.6 cm).

---

## 3. Dates and Information

- **Date:** In domestic correspondence, use the format `day.month.year` (e.g., 4.11.2009). No supplementary zeros are used.
- **Contact information:** The organization's name, address (PO Box/street address), phone, fax, email, website, Business ID, and registered office are usually placed in the header or footer in columns according to the standard.

---

## 4. End Signatures

Space is left at the end of the document for signatures and possible additional information:

1. **Closing greeting:** 2 line breaks after the text.
2. **Publisher/Sender:** 2 line breaks after the greeting, written in CAPITAL LETTERS.
3. **Signature and name clarification:** 3–5 line breaks after the sender's name. The profession/title is written under the name clarification.
4. **Additional markings:** The words *Attachment*, *Distribution*, and *For information* are written at least one line apart from the name clarification.
    - Listed attachments are written with a capital first letter.

---

## 5. Footer

- There must be at least 3 blank lines before the footer.
- Information is usually written with a smaller font than the main text.
