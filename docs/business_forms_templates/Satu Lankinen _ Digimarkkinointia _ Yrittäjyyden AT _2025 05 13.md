# Digital Marketing Basics for Entrepreneurs

Digital marketing is continuous work aimed at making the company discoverable, memorable and facilitating sales.

## 1. Websites and SEO
* **Homepage:** The company's digital business card. Minimum content: Front page, services, prices and contact information.
* **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):** Use keywords in headings, main text and ALT text for images.
* **Responsive design:** The website must function seamlessly on mobile phones and tablets.

## 2. Content Creation
* **Authenticity:** Be yourself. Stories evoke emotions and build trust.

## 3. Customer Information and Data Protection (GDPR)
* **Lead generation:** Contact forms, guides and newsletter subscriptions.
* **Privacy notice:** Mandatory document that explains what information is collected and how it is stored.
* **Security:** Store information behind passwords, don't leave paper lists on the desk.

## 4. Marketing Channels
* Choose channels based on where your customers are. You don't need to be everywhere.
* **Google My Business:** Important for local visibility.
* **Newsletters:** Effective way to maintain long-term customer relationships.

---
**Remember:** Do something, see what interests you, learn and improve. It's better to be different than perfect.
