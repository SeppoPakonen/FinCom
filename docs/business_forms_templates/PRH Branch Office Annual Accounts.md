# PRH: Branch Office Annual Accounts

A foreign company's branch office located in Finland must report its annual accounts to the Trade Register annually.

## 1. Schedule
* **Deadline:** Within 6 months of the end of the financial year.
* **EEA exception:** If the parent company's home country law (EEA country) allows a longer period, that is followed.

## 2. Documents to Report
* **EU regulation compliant:** If the parent company follows EU annual accounts standards, the parent company's official annual accounts are reported (translated to an EU language).
* **Others:** If the parent company does not follow EU standards, the branch office's own annual accounts prepared according to Finnish law must be reported.

## 3. Notification
* **Method:** Electronically in the YTJ service.
* **Signatory:** The branch office's representative or trade name signer.
* **Fee:** Free of charge.

---
**Note:** All attachments are public. Do not include personal identity codes or sensitive information in them.
