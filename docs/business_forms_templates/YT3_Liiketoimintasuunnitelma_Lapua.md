# Template: Business Plan (Growth-oriented Company)

This template is intended for companies planning broader operations or significant investments.

## 1. Business Idea and Vision
* **Summary:** What do you do and for whom?
* **Value proposition:** What unique benefit do you create?
* **Vision:** Situation after 5 years.

## 2. Market Analysis
* **Industry:** Growth prospects and trends.
* **Competition:** Key competitors and their strategies.
* **Market share:** Target position in the market.

## 3. Strategic Choices
* **Competitive strategy:** How do you differentiate? (Price, quality, innovation).
* **Growth strategy:** How is the business expanded?

## 4. Organization and Resources
* **Personnel:** Required expertise and team structure.
* **Production:** Premises, equipment and technology.
* **Partners:** Subcontractors and strategic networks.

## 5. Finance and Profitability
* **Investment plan:** What is needed and what does it cost?
* **Financing:** How are investments covered?
* **Profitability targets:** Revenue and operating profit (EBIT).

## 6. Risk Management
* **Analysis:** Most significant risks and their probability.
* **Management methods:** How are risks anticipated?
