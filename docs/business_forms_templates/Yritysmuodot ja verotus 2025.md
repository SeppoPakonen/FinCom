# Comparison: Business Forms and Taxation 2025

The choice of company form affects your liability, decision-making, profit distribution and taxation.

## 1. Private entrepreneur (Business name)
* **Suitable for:** Single entrepreneurs, small-scale operations.
* **Liability:** Full personal liability with all property.
* **Taxation:** Income is taxed as the entrepreneur's personal earned and capital income.
* **Establishment:** Easy and affordable (approx. 70 €).

## 2. Personal partnerships (General partnership and Limited partnership)
* **General partnership (Ay):** At least two partners. All have unlimited and joint liability. Requires strong trust.
* **Limited partnership (Ky):** At least one general partner (liable with all property) and one silent partner (investor, liability only with invested amount).
* **Taxation:** Income is distributed to partners for taxation according to the partnership agreement.

## 3. Limited liability company (Oy)
* **Suitable for:** Companies of all sizes, professional growth.
* **Liability:** The company itself is liable for its debts. Shareholder risk is limited to the invested capital.
* **Decision-making:** General meeting and board of directors. Share capital is not mandatory (0 €).
* **Taxation:** Independent taxpayer (corporate tax 20 %). Funds to owners as salary or dividends.

## 4. Light entrepreneurship
* Not an actual business form, but a way to invoice for work without your own business ID (through a service provider). Good way to test a business idea.

---
**Tip:** Choose a form that best protects you from the risks of your operations. Limited liability company is often the safest choice for growing business operations.
