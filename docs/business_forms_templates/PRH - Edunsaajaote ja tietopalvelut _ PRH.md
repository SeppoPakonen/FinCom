# PRH: Beneficial Owner Statement and Information Services

PRH provides information about beneficial owners registered in the Trade Register through beneficial owner statements.

## 1. What is a beneficial owner statement?
* Contains the beneficial owner information reported by a single company.
* Available in Finnish, Swedish and English.
* **Cost:** The statement is a paid document.

## 2. Information Publicity and Restrictions
* Beneficial owner information is **not public** in the same way as other Trade Register information.
* Information is only disclosed to parties that have a legitimate purpose according to the Money Laundering Act (e.g. banks, lawyers, authorities).
* **Public information:** Information on *whether* a company has made a beneficial owner notification is public and visible in the Virre service.

## 3. Obtaining a Requested Statement
A beneficial owner statement can be ordered:
1. As a single document order from PRH's web service.
2. As a subscriber to the Virre information service.

---
**Search:** You can check the notification status with a Business ID in the [Virre service](https://virre.prh.fi).
