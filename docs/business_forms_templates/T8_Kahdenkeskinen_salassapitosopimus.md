# T8: Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (Bilateral)

This is a template for a mutual non-disclosure agreement (Mutual NDA) where both parties share and receive confidential information.

---

**PARTIES**
1. [Party A: Name and Business ID]
2. [Party B: Name and Business ID]

**1. SUBJECT OF THE AGREEMENT**
The parties intend to cooperate regarding [Description of cooperation, e.g. software development project or marketing cooperation]. For this reason, the parties will exchange confidential information with each other.

**2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION**
Confidential information includes all information that a party discloses to the other party electronically, orally or in writing and that is marked as confidential or whose nature is clearly such (e.g. trade secrets, technology, customer information).

**3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE PARTIES**
Each party commits to:
* Protecting information received from the other party with at least the same level of care as their own corresponding information.
* Using the information only for the purpose described in section 1 of this agreement.
* Not disclosing the information to third parties without the written consent of the other party.

**4. LIMITATIONS**
The confidentiality obligation does not apply to information that:
* Was already known by the receiving party before this agreement.
* Is publicly available without violating this agreement.
* Was received legally from a third party without a confidentiality obligation.

**5. AGREEMENT PENALTY**
A breach of this agreement results in a penalty of [Amount] euros per breach. In addition, the aggrieved party has the right to full compensation for damages.

**6. APPLICABLE LAW AND DISPUTES**
This agreement is governed by Finnish law. Disputes are primarily resolved through negotiations and secondarily in [District Court name] District Court.
