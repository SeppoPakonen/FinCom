# Articles of Association for a Limited Liability Company (Template)

This is a template for the articles of association of a limited liability company, which includes a **redemption clause**. The redemption clause can restrict the transfer of shares to outsiders.

### 3. Board of Directors
- The company's board of directors consists of 1–5 regular members. If there are fewer than three members, at least one alternate member must be elected.
- The term of office of the board ends at the conclusion of the next regular shareholders' meeting following the election.

### 4. Auditors
- One auditor and one alternate auditor are elected for the company. If a KHT- or HTM-association is elected as auditor, an alternate auditor does not need to be elected.

### 5. Invitation to Shareholders' Meeting
- The invitation to the regular and extraordinary shareholders' meeting must be delivered in writing to shareholders at the address recorded in the shareholder register at least one week before the meeting.

### 6. Regular Shareholders' Meeting
The shareholders' meeting is held annually on a date determined by the board within six months of the end of the financial year. At the meeting:
- The financial statements and auditor's report are presented.
- A resolution is made on approving the profit and loss statement and balance sheet.
- A resolution is made on granting discharge from liability to the members of the board and the managing director.
- A resolution is made on the use of profits.
- Members of the board and the auditor are elected.

### 7. Redemption Clause
If a share is transferred to someone other than the company's current shareholders, the transferee must notify the board immediately. Current shareholders have the right to redeem the transferring share under the following conditions:
- **Restrictions:** The right of redemption does not apply to acquisitions based on inheritance, will, or matrimonial property rights.
- **Division:** If multiple shareholders wish to redeem, shares are divided according to the proportion of share ownership.
- **Redemption Price:** The price agreed upon by the parties, however, at most the value of the share based on the latest financial statements. In gratuitous acquisition, the price is the value based on the financial statements.
- **Time:** The redemption claim must be submitted to the company within one month of when the board was notified of the share transfer.
