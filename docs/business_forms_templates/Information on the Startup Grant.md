# Information on the Startup Grant

The startup grant is a personal support for a starting entrepreneur to secure livelihood during the initial phase of business operations.

## 1. Requirements for Granting
* **Primary occupation:** Entrepreneurship must be the primary occupation.
* **Qualifications:** The applicant must have sufficient expertise or education in the field.
* **Necessity:** The support must be necessary for the entrepreneur's livelihood (not granted if the entrepreneur already receives other income, e.g., salary).
* **Profitability:** The company must have the prerequisites for ongoing profitable operations.
* **Competition situation:** The support must not distort local competition.

## 2. Amount and Duration of Support
* **Amount:** Same as the basic daily allowance (37.21 €/day in 2024–2026).
* **Payment:** Paid for a maximum of five days per calendar week.
* **Duration:** Usually 6 months at a time, maximum 12 months.
* **Taxation:** The startup grant is taxable income for the entrepreneur.

## 3. Application
* **When:** The application must be submitted **before** starting business operations.
* **Where:** Applied through the Employment and Economic Development Office (TE services or municipal trial).
* **Attachments:** Business plan (BP), profitability calculations, and tax debt certificate.

---
**Important:** Do not register the company or start operations before receiving a positive startup grant decision!
