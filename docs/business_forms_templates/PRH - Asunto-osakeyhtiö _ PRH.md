# PRH: Establishing a Housing Company

A housing company (as-oy) is created by registering it in the Trade Register. New companies are automatically included in the apartment information system.

## 1. Schedule and Responsible Parties
* **Deadline:** The establishment notification must be made within **3 months** of signing the articles of incorporation. Delaying causes the establishment to lapse.
* **Signatory:** One of the board members.

## 2. Costs
* **Establishment notification:** 400 €.
* **Additional business name:** 75 € / name.

## 3. Required Attachments
1. Original, signed articles of incorporation.
2. Board members' and property manager's declaration of compliance with the law.
3. Auditor's certificate of payment of shares.
4. Possible attachments to the articles of association (e.g. liability distribution table).
5. Copies of identity documents for persons without a Finnish personal identity code.

## 4. Notification (YTJ service)
* Notification is made electronically at [asiointi.ytj.fi](https://asiointi.ytj.fi/ytjap).
* The articles of association are submitted in electronic form. Sections 1–4 are standard form and the service creates them based on the provided information.
* The shareholder list is transmitted directly from the service to the National Land Survey.

## 5. Monitoring
* The Business ID is received immediately after submission.
* Processing can be monitored in the [Virre service](https://virre.prh.fi).

---
**Note!** Do not attach documents containing sensitive information (e.g. health information) or home addresses unless specifically requested.
