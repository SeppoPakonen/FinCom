# Template: Housing Company Articles of Association

The articles of association of a housing company is a public document that defines the management of the company and apartments.

## Key Sections of the Articles of Association

### Section 1 Trade Name
The company's name is: [Name given in the YTJ service].

### Section 2 Registered Office
The company's registered office is: [Municipality].

### Section 3 Business Activity (Real Estate)
Information about the real estate managed by the company (property ID, name, and basis of management).

### Section 4 Apartment Specifications
Defines which shares provide the right to manage specific apartments or parking spaces.
* Example: *Building A, Apartment A1, 37 m², shares 1–10.*

### Section 5 Signing the Trade Name
How the company name is signed (e.g., board members together or chairman alone).

### Section 6 Charges
Shareholders must pay maintenance and capital charges to cover the company's expenses.
* **Basis:** [E.g., square meters or number of shares].
* **Water charge:** Often collected based on consumption.

### Section 7 Board of Directors
The company's affairs are handled by the board (1–5 regular members). The term of office ends at the next annual general meeting.

### Section 8 General Meeting
Must be held within 6 months of the end of the financial year. The meeting handles:
* Confirming the financial statements.
* Granting discharge from liability.
* Board's statement on future maintenance needs (5-year plan).

---
**Note:** Companies established before 2019 can change their articles of association to digital structured format.
