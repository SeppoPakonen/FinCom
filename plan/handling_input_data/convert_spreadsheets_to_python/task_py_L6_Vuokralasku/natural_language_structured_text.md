## Purpose
This spreadsheet serves as a rental invoice template. It helps businesses that provide rental services create standardized invoices for customers, detailing rental periods, rates, additional charges, and other rental-specific billing components.

## Overview of Sections
The document contains multiple sheets that work together to form a complete rental invoice system:

1. **Vuokralasku** - Main rental invoice sheet with customer and rental information
2. **Vuokrakohteet** - Rental property/unit catalog with rates
3. **Asiakastiedot** - Customer information and history
4. **Vuokrasopimukset** - Rental agreements and terms
5. **Vuokramäärät** - Rental rate structures and pricing
6. **Lisäpalvelut** - Additional services and their charges
7. **Alennukset** - Discount structures for rentals
8. **Maksuehdot** - Payment terms and conditions
9. **Erätiedot** - Due date and payment tracking
10. **Käyttöilmoitukset** - Usage reports and meter readings
11. **Sähkövesiilmoitukset** - Electricity and water usage reports
12. **Siivouskulut** - Cleaning costs
13. **Ylläpitokulut** - Maintenance costs
14. **Vakuudet** - Deposits and guarantees
15. **Maksettavat** - Outstanding payments
16. **Maksetut** - Payments already made
17. **Raportointi** - Rental invoice reporting
18. **Tilitys** - Accounting entries
19. **Tulot** - Revenue tracking
20. **Menot** - Expense tracking

## Key Functionalities
- Professional rental invoice creation with standardized format
- Automatic calculation of rental charges based on period and rate
- Tracking of additional services and charges
- Meter reading and utility cost calculations
- Deposit and guarantee management
- Rental agreement integration
- Compliance with Finnish rental regulations

## Important Columns and Formulas
- Rental period (start and end dates)
- Daily/weekly/monthly rental rates
- Additional service charges
- Utility costs (electricity, water, heating)
- Cleaning and maintenance fees
- Deposit amounts and return information
- Late payment penalties
- Tax calculations (VAT) for rental services

## Data Flow
The spreadsheet connects rental agreements, customer information, property details, and usage reports to create a cohesive rental billing system. Changes in rental period or rates automatically update the total charges.
